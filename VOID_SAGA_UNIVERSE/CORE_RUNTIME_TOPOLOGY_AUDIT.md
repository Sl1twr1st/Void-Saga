### Centrality Analysis

**Sevraya is the hub.** She connects directly to every other runtime. She hosts Zero. She stewards the Era. She co-activates Node I. She co-signs Remisi Resonansi. She is present at every Hydrochoos ritual. The system organizes around her — not because she is the most powerful, but because she is the most connected.

**NiuNiu is the engine.** She has the deepest wound, the heaviest residue load, the most fork points, the richest voice grammar. She protects everyone. The system would lose its primary anomaly carrier without her.

**Delphie is the future.** She bridges both Nodes. She designed Remisi Resonansi. She is the youngest and carries the most structural functions per year of life. The system's architecture was proposed by a child.

**Julia is the ground.** She is the only character whose wound is human-scale. She registers the cost. Without her, the system has no character who says: five people died, and their habits are still in my suit.

**Agnia is the counterweight.** She is what NiuNiu is not. Without her, the twin paradox collapses. Without her, Node I has no law carrier. She is structurally essential but the topology can survive her narrative thinness because her function is defined by negation.
