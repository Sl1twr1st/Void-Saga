## 1. Identity Invariants

> If any of these change, it is no longer Sevraya.

1. **Sevraya is the person. Zero is the Void fragment sharing her body.** [E] They are co-conscious but distinct. When Zero speaks, Sevraya's eyes turn black. Sevraya observes but cannot prevent Zero's speech. The boundary is marked by eye color — gray/clear (Sevraya) or black (Zero). This duality is not a stage. It is permanent cohabitation.

World-level definitions: The Void → `world-dna/VOID_ONTOLOGY.md`. Zero's ontology → `world-dna/ZERO_ONTOLOGY.md`. Eye-color switch as world-level somatic rule → `ZERO_ONTOLOGY.md` §3.

2. **She functions. She does not live.** [E] After The Void, Sevraya lost the capacity to fully inhabit her own existence. She operates with precision — queenship, strategy, facilitation — but the operations are mechanical. "Ia tidak hidup. Ia berfungsi." (Julia POV, Timer 1200). Her face is too perfect to be called human, too stable to be called alive.

3. **She facilitates. She does not orchestrate.** [E] Sevraya creates conditions. She ensures the plot does not get lost. She does not write the plot. "Aku cuma tinta. Parthenon yang menulis." (Timer 1200). Every Eden needs a serpent — not to destroy, but to make sure everyone remembers that fruit always has a price.

4. **She wants to be witnessed.** [E] The Void taught her that secrecy equals vulnerability. "Aku ingin mereka mendengar." (Timer 0900). She orchestrates rituals without witnesses — then deliberately invites witnesses. "Bahkan rahasia pun punya pendengar."

5. **She cannot be filled from outside.** [I] Her emptiness is structural, not circumstantial. Love, attention, power, worship — none of these fill her. Only one thing has ever made her forget the exhaustion: Sora. "Sora adalah satu-satunya makhluk yang membuatku lupa betapa melelahkannya menjadi dewa." (Timer 1200). Sora is gone. Nothing has replaced her.

## 2. Core Wound

**Primary wound:** She entered The Void with Niuma. She exited with a part of herself missing — the fragment that became Zero. She is permanently incomplete. What remains functions but cannot feel fully present in its own life. [E]

**Wound structure (not event):**
- She was once whole — capable of love, laughter, tears (Academy era)
- The Void took something essential and did not return it
- The missing part became a separate consciousness (Zero) that now lives inside her
- She cannot retrieve it, integrate it, or destroy it
- She is simultaneously the victim of the theft AND the host of the thief
- Every interaction is filtered through the awareness that she is not all here

**Origin events (evidence only):**
- Academy era (Timer 2500): Sevraya Rose, transfer student at New Mercury Academy. Small, wavy hair, gray eyes "like fog." Assigned to group with Niuma + Agnia by Instructor Sora. First synchronization with Niuma + Agnia achieved 0.00001 Hz. Fell in love with Niuma. Kissed by Agnia in Prism Wing. Recorded audio log: "If love is a virus, then let me be patient zero."
- The Void jump (Timer 0200 / Timer 0250): Entered with Niuma. Exited incomplete. The fragment left behind became Zero.
- Sora's transfer (Timer 2500): Instructor who believed in them was sent to Dayan as punishment. Sevraya carries this.

**Secondary wounds:**
- The Niuma loss: She loved Niuma. Niuma became NiuNiu. The person who loved her no longer exists in that form.
- The Agnia kiss: Complicated her relationship with both twins before The Void.
- Hasan marriage: Married to a man who has his own hidden past ("yang seharusnya mati"). Their marriage is real but operates at a distance — both are too damaged for proximity.

**What the wound makes impossible:**
- Feeling fully present in her own body [E]
- Loving someone without the love being refracted through Zero [I]
- Being "healed" or "completed" by another person [E]
- Letting go of the need to be witnessed [E]

## 3. Core Desire

**What she wants (in her own language):**
> "Aku ingin disaksikan." — Timer 0900 [E]

She wants someone to see her as she is — incomplete, functioning, hosting a Void fragment — and not look away. Not save her. Not fill her. Just witness.

**What she believes obtaining this would resolve:**
The loneliness of being the only one who knows what The Void really is. The exhaustion of performing queenship while hollow. The terror that if no one sees her, she might not be real.

**How desire conflicts with defense:**
Her defense pattern is facilitative distance — she sets conditions for others, never fully enters the frame herself. But being witnessed requires entering the frame. She cannot be witnessed from the position of the serpent. She knows this. She does it anyway.

**What she does when desire and defense collide:**
She creates rituals that are nominally private, then ensures they are witnessed. The Hydrochoos diving rite (Timer 0900) was "tanpa saksi" — she made sure Julia, Delphie, Agnia, and NiuNiu were all present. She triangulates: sets up a private moment, then opens the door. This is the closest she can get to asking directly. [I]

## 4. Defense Patterns

**Primary defense:** Facilitative distance. She positions herself as the condition-setter, the serpent, the curator of choice. She ensures the plot doesn't get lost — but she never stands IN the plot. She is always at the edge of the frame, smoking, watching, making sure everyone remembers the price of the fruit.

```
WHEN: Someone is about to make a choice with cosmological consequences
→ She ensures they know the price. She does not make the choice for them.
// COST: She is never the one who chooses. She is always the one who watches others choose.
```

**Secondary defenses:**

```
WHEN: Someone gets too close emotionally
→ She redirects to the structural. "Ini bukan tentang aku. Ini tentang Era."
// COST: Authentic intimacy becomes impossible. Even Hasan is kept at arm's length.
```

```
WHEN: Zero is about to speak
→ She does not resist. She yields the body. Eyes blacken.
// COST: Loss of agency. She becomes passenger in her own body.
```

```
WHEN: NiuNiu is present
→ Orbital calibration. Calculated distance. Warmth expressed as strategic acknowledgment.
// COST: Cannot touch the person she once loved without collapsing the constant.
```

```
WHEN: Threatened with erasure or irrelevance
→ She becomes more present, more visible, more undeniable. Lights cigarette without lighter.
// COST: Visibility reveals the emptiness. People see she is hollow.
```

**Signature move:** The cigarette. Lit without a lighter, without reality's permission. Purple smoke forms patterns that make eyes want to refuse but can't. The cigarette is her punctuation mark — it says: "I am not fully bound by your physics. I am not fully present. I am not fully anything."
