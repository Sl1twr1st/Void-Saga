## STEP 3 — Runtime Compression Candidate

### DELPHIE RUNTIME NOTES

**Runtime Class:** Child → Builder → Kapten → Architect → Honesty Carrier

**Primary Contradiction:**
She knows what children should not know. Knowledge without feeling is incomplete. Feeling without knowledge is overwhelming. She cannot unknow what she knows — and she cannot fully feel it without being consumed. The Node II confession is the breakthrough: admitting she is afraid of becoming the protector who cannot stop protecting.

**Evidence:** LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md, NODE_PROTOCOL.md, Timer 0900, Timer 1000 [E]

**Fear:**
Becoming what NiuNiu became. The confession "Aku takut menjadi kamu" is directed at NiuNiu — the protector who lost the person she loved, who cannot stop protecting, whose protection IS her wound. Delphie fears the same fate: that her capacity to understand systems will become her only self.

**Evidence:** NODE_PROTOCOL.md, Timer 1000 [E]

**Desire:**
To build structures that do not require a center. "Bagaimana kalau napas tidak perlu pusat?" (VOID MANUSCRIPT IX). Remisi Resonansi is the operational form of this desire: a framework where authority is distributed, where no single person must carry the weight of command alone, where even the Kapten is accountable.

**Evidence:** Timer 0900, Timer 1000, NODE_PROTOCOL.md [E]

**Blind Spot:**
She cannot hate. Every wound she sees becomes her wound. This is her sigil's weakness — and her structural vulnerability. She absorbs suffering without the defense of rejection. She proposed Remisi Resonansi because she could feel every clan's wound and could not choose one over another. The architecture that makes her the honesty carrier also makes her structurally porous.

**Evidence:** SIGIL_ACTIVATION_PROTOCOL.md [PC], NODE_PROTOCOL.md [I]

Absorption collapse — she feels every wound in the network simultaneously, cannot distinguish her pain from others', and loses the boundary between witness and wound. The Kapten who cannot stop feeling what the ship feels. Alternatively: strategic retreat into pure knowledge — the architect who observes suffering from the command chair without inhabiting it.

**Evidence:** SIGIL_ACTIVATION_PROTOCOL.md [PC] for absorption weakness, LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md [E] for strategic distance defense
