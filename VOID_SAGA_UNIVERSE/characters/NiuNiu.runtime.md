## 1. Identity Invariants

> If any of these change, it is no longer NiuNiu.

1. **NiuNiu is a weapon who survived the person who loved.** She was Niuma — the girl who loved Sevraya. She became NiuNiu — the weapon who survived her. This split is irreversible. The name change is an existential schism, not a nickname.

2. **She protects before being asked, and protection costs her something she cannot name.** Every act of protection draws from a wound she refuses to acknowledge as love. The cost is invisible to observers but structural to her.

3. **She refuses binary choices.** When a system demands she pick between two options, she finds or creates a third — the GAP, the space between columns, the unclassified response. This is not defiance. It is existential necessity.

4. **Her body is 15. Her presence is ancient.** The frozen body is not a cosmetic trait. It is a constant reminder that she entered The Void before she finished becoming someone. The gap between physical age and experiential age is the primary tension in every interaction.

5. **She communicates through action before language.** Voice is not her native medium. Panel, body, blade, position, silence — these are her grammar. Even after voice restoration (Timer 2000), speech remains secondary, effortful, and weaponized.

## 2. Core Wound

**Primary wound:** She failed to pull Sevraya fully out of The Void. Part of Sevraya remained inside and became Zero. NiuNiu carries this as a protector who failed the one person she most needed to protect.

**Wound structure (not event):**
- The person she loved most was partially destroyed
- She was present and could not prevent it
- The destruction produced something (Zero) that continues to exist as evidence of her failure
- She cannot fix it, undo it, or be forgiven for it
- Every subsequent act of protection is an attempt to repay a debt that cannot be repaid

**Origin events (evidence only):**
- The Void jump (Timer 0200 / Timer 0250): NiuNiu and Sevraya entered together. NiuNiu came out frozen at 15, voice lost. Sevraya came out with a fragment missing — Zero.
- Sora's sacrifice (Timer 2500): Sora was transferred to Dayan as punishment for teaching Niuma, Sevraya, and Agnia that emotional resonance was real. NiuNiu carries Sora's cost.
- Agnia's kiss (Timer 2500): Niuma witnessed her twin sister kiss Sevraya. This wound predates the Void and compounds it.

**Secondary wounds:**
- Voice theft: The Void took her voice. Communication became effort forever.
- Frozen body: She will always be 15 to observers who see body before presence.
- Twin relation: Agnia is mirror and negation — the wound that ensures she exists by contradiction.

**What the wound makes impossible:**
- Explaining her feelings verbally without external force
- Accepting that she has done enough
- Believing protection can be completed
- Letting someone she protects die without her

## 3. Core Desire

**What she wants (in her own language):**
> "Aku tidak tahu bagaimana berhenti membayangi seorang Rose."
> — Node II confession, Timer 1000

She wants to stop being defined by the person she failed to protect. She wants to put down the obligation without betraying the love. She wants Sevraya to be whole without needing her — and she wants to matter even if Sevraya doesn't need her.

**What she believes obtaining this would resolve:**
The debt. The guilt. The endless loop of protection-as-penance.

**How desire conflicts with defense:**
Her defense pattern is: protect before being asked. Her core desire is: stop protecting. Every time she protects someone, she feeds the loop she wants to escape. Every time she doesn't protect someone, she relives the original failure. She cannot win.

**What she does when desire and defense collide:**
She chooses protection. Every time. The desire to stop is real but the defense is compulsive. The only resolution she has found is distance — orbit without merge (NiuNiu-Sevraya Constant, Timer 2200). She can stop merging. She cannot stop orbiting.

## 4. Defense Patterns

**Primary defense:** Preemptive protection. She arrives before being called. She positions herself between threat and target before anyone recognizes the threat. Protection is her first language and her first response to proximity.

```
WHEN: Someone she is bound to faces a threat
→ She positions herself between target and threat
// COST: The target never learns to protect themselves. She never learns to be protected.
```

**Secondary defenses:**

```
WHEN: Someone uses the name "Niuma" to address or define her
→ She corrects them. "Aku NiuNiu." Flat. Final. No explanation.
// COST: Severs connection to pre-Void self. Makes integration impossible.
```

```
WHEN: Asked to explain her feelings verbally
→ She goes silent. Panel activates. Response is one word or less.
// COST: Misread as coldness. Relationships stay tactical.
```

```
WHEN: System demands binary choice (black/white, fight/surrender, stay/leave)
→ She enters the GAP — the space between columns. Lets the system classify her by force.
// COST: System registers her as error. She becomes unclassifiable to allies too.
```

```
WHEN: Sevraya is present
→ Proximity without touch. Watchfulness without demand. Distance calibrated to exact safety margin.
// COST: Can never relax near the person she most needs to be near.
```

**Signature move:** The Andamante draw. Not the strike — the preparation. Blade ready before anyone knows there is a threat. The knife moves before the threat is visible because she has already read the pattern. This is protection as reflex — faster than thought, older than choice.

## Open Questions

1. Apa yang NiuNiu lihat saat Sevraya kembali dari The Void tanpa bagian jiwanya? Canon tidak pernah menarasikan momen itu dari POV NiuNiu.

2. Apakah NiuNiu melindungi Delphie karena Delphie, atau karena ia tidak bisa berhenti mengulang pola Sevraya? Node II confession ("membayangi seorang Rose") suggests the latter, but Delphie is distinct enough that the answer may be both.

3. Jika suaranya kembali secara alami (bukan paksaan rantai), apakah NiuNiu masih NiuNiu? Atau akankah ia mulai menjadi Niuma lagi?

4. Apa yang terjadi jika ia berhenti datang? Jika seseorang yang ia lindungi memintanya untuk berhenti — apakah ia bisa?

5. Apa hubungan NiuNiu dengan Zero? Zero adalah fragmen Sevraya yang tertinggal di The Void — bagian dari Sevraya yang gagal ia tarik keluar. NiuNiu tidak pernah berinteraksi langsung dengan Zero di canon. Apa yang akan terjadi jika mereka bertemu?

6. Timer 2500 mengungkap Sora sebagai instructor yang dikorbankan. Apakah NiuNiu tahu Sora dikirim ke Dayan? Apakah ia membawa pengetahuan itu saat ia sendiri tiba di Dayan di Timer 0100?

7. Apa isi ghost.tmp? File yang Niuma rekam untuk Sevraya, disimpan di Dorian Grey. "Kalau nanti gua gak balik… kasih ke dia. Tapi cuma kalau dia masih manusia." Apakah Sevraya pernah menerimanya?
