# Bab 00 — Fork Questions

> Read by `./scripts/start-fork`. Lines that begin with a number + `. ` are asked, one at a time.
> Plain language. A stranger must be able to answer these without asking Jali anything.
> Keep each question on a single line (the CLI reads line by line).
> ONE file. Not an intent library. Do not add variants here — that was the relapse.

## Questions

1. Which moment do you want to change? (a) Bug Janggal — LO first approaches GUA at 00:03; (b) Folder Error — they sync independently; (c) Genesis Error — the birth of VoidOS. Pick one letter and say why that moment.
2. In one sentence: what if...? Describe your change in your own words.
3. Be specific — does the character ignore, refuse, not notice, or something else? Say which.
4. What is at stake in that moment? What shifts if your change happens?
5. How long does the change last — only that moment, or does it ripple forward permanently?
6. What is the immediate consequence — what happens right after?
7. Does the rest of Bab 00 still happen? (the timestamp bug still gets fixed, genesis.txt still gets written, the stealth project still starts) Say what survives and what breaks.
