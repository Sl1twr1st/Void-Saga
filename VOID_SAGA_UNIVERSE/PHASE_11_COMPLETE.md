## Assessment

Phase 11 is complete. The protocol layer that was a pending list in CLAUDE.md is now a documented architecture of six protocols connected by a six-phase boot sequence. Every protocol traces the Goetic chain. Every cost has a consequence. Every consequence has a residue. No ontology leaks.

The architecture's strongest structural contribution is the boot sequence — the discovery that Void Entry, Sigil, Living Chain, Node, Paradox, and Zero Node form a single dependency chain where each phase consumes the output of the previous phase. This was not stated in canon. It was inferred from canon events and verified against every available source. It is marked `[I]` throughout.

Two protocols remain pending: ZERO_NODE (entity incubating, protocol premature) and ERA_TRANSITION (single observed instance). Both are documented in their structural position; neither blocks the architecture.

Phase 12 — Character Runtime Completion — is the logical next phase. Eleven runtimes pending. The three highest-priority runtimes (Julia, Delphie, Agnia) are participants in 3+ protocols each. Their behavior is referenced by existing protocol documents but not yet compressed into runtime form.
