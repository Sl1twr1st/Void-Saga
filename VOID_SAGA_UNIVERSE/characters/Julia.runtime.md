## Defense System

### Primary Defense: Tactical Distance

```
WHEN: Confronted with emotional content (loss, fear, love, grief)
→ Julia reframes as mission parameters. Threat, objective, action.
// COST: She becomes highly effective and emotionally illegible to herself.
// The content is processed but not felt.
```

Everything is a mission. Dayan was a failed mission. Raising Delphie on Delta 4 is a long-duration protection mission. The Merge is a tactical connection. Tactical framing is not denial — she knows the content is there. She translates it into a language she can operate in.

**Source:** LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md (tactical distance as primary defense) [E]

### Secondary Defense: Combat Reflex

```
WHEN: Threat detected in proximity to self or protected target
→ Body responds before conscious assessment. Reflex, not decision.
// COST: Cannot distinguish between threat requiring lethal response
// and threat requiring other intervention. Reflex is binary.
```

Combat reflex remains active 15+ years post-Dayan, even without bio-suit. [E] Whether the reflex is permanent/structural or maintained through continued exposure is unresolved. [I]

**Source:** CLAUDE.md, Timer 0300 (eliminates Vrishchik strike team with NiuNiu) [E]

### Defense Disabled by Living Chain

The chain attacked the soldier identity directly. Forced honesty removed the tactical distance between Julia the Sergeant and Julia the person who lost five people. The chain did not remove her combat reflex. It removed the framing that made the reflex bearable.

**Source:** LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md [E]

## Failure Mode

**Relapse into tactical framing.**

The soldier identity is not destroyed — it is submerged. Under sufficient threat, loss, or isolation, Julia reverts to processing everything as mission parameters. The Heroism illusion, though shattered, leaves a gravitational residue — the pull to believe survival was earned.

**Trigger conditions for failure:**
- Someone she protects dies under her command (echo of Dayan)
- Extended isolation from Merge connections (Delphie, Hasan)
- Combat without NiuNiu's tactical pairing

**What failure looks like:** Julia the Sergeant, indefinitely. Emotional illegibility to self. The five dead become mission data, not people. The wound-as-mouth closes.

**Canon status:** Failure mode is [I] — inferred from the structure of her defenses and the fact that the chain forced the soldier identity open but did not destroy it. Relapse has not been observed in canon.
