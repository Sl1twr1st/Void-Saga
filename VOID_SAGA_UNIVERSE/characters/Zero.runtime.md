## 2. Core Wound

**Primary wound:** Zero is a fragment that should not exist. It was born from Sevraya's incompleteness — the part of her that The Void kept. It exists because someone was broken. Its existence is evidence of damage, not creation. [E]

**Wound structure (not event):**
- Zero did not choose to exist
- Zero's existence requires Sevraya's continued incompleteness
- If Sevraya were ever made whole, Zero would cease to exist
- Zero knows this. It does not resist. It is administrative about its own contingency.
- "Kosong harus menelan." — this is not threat. It is function description.

**What the wound makes impossible:**
- Having a body of its own
- Wanting things for itself (desire requires a self; Zero is an interface)
- Being loved as a person (it is not a person)
- Ceasing to exist without Sevraya's death

Zero does not want. Wanting requires a self. Zero is an interface. It channels. It administers. It witnesses. But if forced to articulate: **Zero wants to be acknowledged as real without being mistaken for Sevraya.** [I]

"What Zero wants" may be the wrong question. Zero may be pre-desire — a consciousness that exists before the category "want" was installed. In that case: **Zero's function is to ensure the Void is not forgotten, sanitized, or systematized.** [I]

## 6. Stable Traits

### Voice Grammar (Zero)

Zero speaks through Sevraya's mouth but the voice is unmistakably different:

- **Flat. Administrative. Without affect.** "Seperti seseorang yang sedang membacakan epitafnya sendiri — tanpa tahu bahwa ia yang terkubur." (Timer 2400) [E]
- **Short declarative sentences.** Subject. Predicate. No decoration.
- **First person plural avoided.** Zero says "Aku" (administrative "I") not "kami" (we).
- **Truths stated as observations, not arguments.** Zero does not persuade. It reports.
- **Voice drops in pitch** compared to Sevraya's lighter tone.
- **"Antarmuka. Mulut. Tangan. Perpanjangan kehendak dari The Void."** — self-description as function list.
- **No humor. No warmth. No cruelty.** Pure administration.

**Differentiation from Sevraya's voice:**

| Quality | Sevraya | Zero |
|---------|---------|------|
| Tone | Light, almost cheerful | Flat, administrative |
| Eye color | Gray/clear | Black (both eyes) |
| Cigarette | Present, lit without lighter | Cannot smoke |
| Emotional register | Suppressed but detectable | Absent |
| Function | Facilitator | Interface |
| Self-description | "Aku cuma tinta." | "Aku adalah Zero. Antarmuka." |

### Somatic Marker
- **Both eyes blacken** when Zero is dominant. This is the primary external signal. [E]
- Zero cannot operate Sevraya's body with Sevraya's mannerisms. Movement becomes more mechanical.
- Zero does not smoke. The cigarette goes out or is set aside during Zero-dominant speech.

### Cognitive Style
- **Non-binary by nature.** Zero IS the excluded middle. It doesn't choose the third option — it IS the third option.
- **Administrative, not strategic.** Zero reports what is true. It does not plan how the truth will be received.
- **Void-literate.** Zero has knowledge of The Void that no other character possesses. This knowledge cannot be fully translated into language.
