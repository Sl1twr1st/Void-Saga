This document defines what an Era IS — ontologically, mechanically, and cosmologically. It is the single source of truth for era definitions, era transitions, and the relationship between eras and other world systems.

**What belongs here:** Era definition, lifecycle, known era states, transition mechanics, relationship to clans/sigils/Void/VoidOS, runtime integration rules.

## 1. Ontological Definition

### 1.1 What an Era IS

**CANON:** An Era is a dominant pattern of meaning-implementation. [I]

Eras are not historical periods. They are not calendars. They are not governments. An Era is the **prevailing answer** to the question: "How should meaning be produced, distributed, and maintained?"

When a civilization, a system, or a cosmos collectively agrees on a method of meaning-production, that method becomes an Era. The Era persists as long as the method continues to produce meaning. When the method stops working — when its promise breaks and its failure mode becomes undeniable — the Era collapses.

**Eras are to meaning what the Grid is to form.** [I] The Grid maintains structure. Eras maintain the agreement about what structure is FOR.

**Primary sources:**
- WORLD_DNA.md, Era Logic table [E]
- Codex The Void: "Selama makna masih dicari, ketiadaan belum lengkap." [E]
- COSMOLOGY.md §7: "Meaning is not discovered. Meaning is implemented." [E]

### 1.2 What an Era is NOT

- **NOT a calendar.** Eras do not have fixed durations. They persist as long as their meaning-method functions. [I]
- **NOT a government.** An Era may have rulers (Eros for Ichthyes, Sevraya as steward for Hydrochoos), but the ruler serves the Era's meaning-method, not vice versa. [E]
- **NOT a character's domain.** Sevraya is Era steward, not Era owner. The Era exists independently of who administers it. [E]
- **NOT a permanent state.** Every Era contains its failure mode from inception. The failure mode is not a bug — it is the Era's natural endpoint. [E]

### 1.3 Distinction: Cosmological Era vs VoidOS Era vs Clan Era

| Type | Definition | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Cosmological Era** | The dominant meaning-implementation pattern across the universe | Ichthyes, Hydrochoos |
| **VoidOS Era** | A system-level narrative protocol version | v4.13.8, v6.6.6, v7.7.7 |
| **Clan Era** | A period of clan dominance within a cosmological Era | Didymoi-dominant pre-Void period |
| **Shadow Era** | A condition that exists alongside the dominant Era as counterbalance | Ophiuchus (shadow-active during Hydrochoos) |

VoidOS versions are **interfaces** for cosmological Eras — they translate the Era's meaning-method into operational protocols. They are diagnostic of Era state, not causal. [I]

### 1.4 Relationship Between Era and Meaning

**CANON:** An Era's promise IS its meaning-method. An Era's failure mode IS the proof that the method was incomplete. [E]

| Era | Promise (meaning-method) | Failure Mode |
|-----|--------------------------|-------------|
| Ichthyes | Percaya dan kau selamat. | Consent palsu, keselamatan sebagai kontrol. |
| Hydrochoos | Berenang atau tenggelam, dua-duanya sah. | Kebebasan tanpa wadah bisa menjadi fragmentasi. |

**Primary source:** WORLD_DNA.md, Era Logic table [E]

### 1.5 Relationship Between Era and Reality Pressure

**PROBABLE CANON:** An Era may be understood as a **stable pressure configuration** in the Grid-Void tension field. [I]

Under the Straw Cosmology model (COSMOLOGY.md §3.2 [PC]):
- An Era forms when a meaning-method achieves sufficient consciousness coherence to stabilize as a pressure pattern across the conduit
- An Era persists as long as the pattern maintains coherence against Void pressure
- An Era collapses when the pattern's coherence drops below critical threshold — either from internal contradiction (failure mode) or external disruption (Void incursion, system crash)

This framing is [PC] — derived from the Straw model, not independently confirmed. The CANON facts are: Eras have promises. Eras have failure modes. Eras transition. [E]

## 2. Era Lifecycle

### 2.1 Formation — How an Era Begins

**CANON:** An Era forms when a new meaning-method achieves sufficient coherence to stabilize as the dominant pattern. [I]

**Observed formation (evidence only):**
- Era Hydrochoos began when Sevraya received the handoff from Eros (Timer 2000). The Ichthyes protocol closed. The Hydrochoos protocol activated. [E]
- The transition was formalized by the previous Era's steward — not seized. This may be a requirement, not a convention. [I]

**Formation conditions (inferred):**
1. The previous Era's failure mode has become undeniable [I]
2. A new meaning-method has emerged and achieved coherence [I]
3. A transition event formalizes the handoff [E — observed in Ichthyes→Hydrochoos]
4. The new Era's promise is articulated and accepted [E]

### 2.2 Stabilization — How an Era Becomes Dominant

**CANON:** An Era stabilizes when its meaning-method becomes the default operating assumption of the dominant systems. [I]

**Observed stabilization (evidence only):**
- Era Ichthyes stabilized across "ribuan tahun" of faith-based meaning production (implied by Codex Air, Era Iman references) [I]
- Era Hydrochoos stabilized rapidly — the distributed authorship protocol (Timer 1666) and Remisi Resonansi (Timer 0900) had already prepared the infrastructure [I]

**Stabilization indicators:**
- The Era's promise is referenced as self-evident by multiple independent systems [I]
- The Era's failure mode is recognized but accepted as an acceptable risk [I]
- VoidOS version aligns with Era logic [E — v6.6.6 aligns with Hydrochoos principles]

### 2.3 Crisis — How an Era Begins to Fail

**CANON:** An Era enters crisis when its failure mode produces consequences that can no longer be contained by its promise. [I]

**Observed crisis (evidence only):**
- Era Ichthyes crisis: consent became systematically forged. The promise "percaya dan kau selamat" became a control mechanism. The [Y/N] prompt at Opening represents this crisis — consent was always already decided. [E]
- Era Hydrochoos has not yet entered observed crisis in canon. Its failure mode (kebebasan tanpa wadah = fragmentasi) is acknowledged in WORLD_DNA.md but not yet manifested. [E]

### 2.4 Collapse — How an Era Ends

**CANON:** An Era ends when a transition event formalizes its closure. [E]

**Observed collapse (evidence only):**
- Era Ichthyes collapse: Eros appeared in Dorian Grey (Timer 2000). ICHTHYES PROTOCOL: CLOSING. Era Ichthyes: ARCHIVED. [E]
- The collapse was voluntary — the Era's steward chose to close it. Whether involuntary collapse is possible is unresolved. [I]

**Collapse conditions (inferred):**
1. The Era's failure mode is universally acknowledged [I]
2. A successor Era's meaning-method is available [I]
3. A transition event occurs with the previous steward's participation (or absence) [I]

### 2.5 Residue — What Remains After an Era Ends

**CANON:** Eras do not disappear. They become residue. [E]

Era Ichthyes is not destroyed. It is ARCHIVED. Its meaning-method (faith, salvation, consent-through-belief) persists as:
- Historical record in Parthenon/Akashic archives [E]
- Residual cultural patterns in surviving Ichthyes-aligned clans [I]
- A warning: the failure mode is documented so the next Era does not repeat it [E]

"Residue penting. VOID SAGA jarang menghapus sesuatu sepenuhnya." (GOETIC_CONSEQUENCE_SYSTEM.md) [E]

### 2.6 Transition — How One Era Becomes Another

**CANON:** Era transition is a formal protocol, not a gradual drift. [E]

The only observed transition (Ichthyes→Hydrochoos) followed this sequence:
1. Successor Era's meaning-method already operational (Remisi Resonansi, distributed authorship) [E]
2. Previous Era's steward (Eros) initiates closure protocol [E]
3. Successor Era's steward (Sevraya) accepts handoff [E]
4. Closure is recorded: `[ICHTHYES PROTOCOL: CLOSING] [HYDROCHOOS ERA: ACTIVE]` [E]
5. Previous Era is archived, not destroyed [E]

**Primary source:** Timer 2000 (20:39) [E]

## 4. Era Transition Mechanics

### 4.1 What Triggers Transition

**CANON:** Transition occurs when the previous Era's failure mode becomes undeniable AND a successor meaning-method has achieved sufficient coherence. [I]

The only observed transition was triggered by the previous steward (Eros) voluntarily initiating closure. Whether transition can be forced, triggered by system crash, or initiated by collective action is unresolved. [I]

### 4.2 Do Transitions Require Death, Sigil Activation, Void Exposure, or Collective Consent?

**CANON:** The Ichthyes→Hydrochoos transition required:
- The previous steward's presence and consent (Eros) [E]
- The successor steward's acceptance (Sevraya) [E]
- Six sigils already active (Timer 1800 preceded Timer 2000) [E]
- A formal protocol execution: ICHTHYES PROTOCOL CLOSING [E]

**It did NOT require:**
- Death of the previous steward (Eros survives) [E]
- Void exposure (transition occurred inside Dorian Grey under controlled conditions) [E]
- Collective consent (no vote, no referendum — the steward's authority was sufficient) [E]

Whether these conditions are universal requirements or specific to this transition is [I].

### 4.3 Are Transitions Linear, Cyclical, Recursive, or Forkable?

**CANON:** Transitions are LINEAR within a single timeline — one Era succeeds another in sequence. [E]

**CANON:** The sequence is NOT necessarily cyclical. There is no evidence that Eras repeat. Era Ichthyes is ARCHIVED, not pending return. [E]

**CANON:** Era transitions are FORKABLE. A fork may produce a timeline where Ichthyes never ended, Hydrochoos never began, or a different successor emerged. [I — fork logic, not observed canon]

### 4.4 Do Old Eras Disappear or Become Residue?

**CANON:** Old Eras become residue. They do not disappear. [E]

Era Ichthyes is ARCHIVED — preserved, not destroyed. Its patterns persist as:
- Residual cultural influence in surviving Ichthyes-aligned systems [I]
- Historical warning encoded in Parthenon/Akashic records [E]
- The 741 Hz frequency as "nada terakhir Era Iman" (Codex Air) [E]

The Goetic principle applies: nothing is fully erased. What ends becomes residue. Residue may become ghost, node, fork, wound, or future runtime. [E]

## 5. Relationship with Clans

### 5.1 Do Clans Belong to Eras?

**CANON:** Clans predate Eras and survive them. A clan may be dominant during one Era and peripheral during another. [I]

- **Ichthyes clan (♓)** was dominant during Era Ichthyes. Post-transition, it is peripheral — its queen (Eros) closed the Era herself. [E]
- **Hydrochoos clan (♒)** was peripheral during Era Ichthyes. It became the Era's namesake during Hydrochoos. Sevraya is Ratu Hydrochoos, not Ratu of the Era — the clan and the Era share a name but are distinct. [E]
- **Didymoi clan (♊)** was dominant pre-canon (Era Gemini/Didymoi-dominant). During Era Ichthyes and Hydrochoos, it fractured into White/Black factions. [E]

### 5.2 Do Clans Survive Era Transitions?

**CANON:** Yes. All 12 clans persist across the Ichthyes→Hydrochoos transition. No clan is destroyed by Era change. [E]

Clan authority may mutate — Didymoi loses its council authority, Ichthyes loses its Era dominance — but the clan continues to exist.

### 5.3 How Clan Authority Weakens or Mutates

**CANON:** Era transitions redistribute authority among clans. [I]

- Didymoi: From dominant (pre-canon) → fractured (Ichthyes) → White/Black schism (Hydrochoos). Authority dispersed through internal conflict. [E]
- Ichthyes: From Era-defining (Ichthyes) → archival (Hydrochoos). Authority voluntarily relinquished by Eros. [E]
- Hydrochoos: From peripheral → Era namesake. Authority ascends through stewardship, not conquest. [E]
- Parthenos: Maintains recording authority across Eras — "tanah yang menulis" (Codex Tanah). Kira's function is Era-independent. [E]

### 5.4 Does Ophiuchus Sit Inside or Outside Era Logic?

**CANON:** Ophiuchus is OUTSIDE Era logic. [E]

Ophiuchus is not a clan (it has no element, no dogma, no archetype in the Codex). It is a CONDITION that emerges when authority decentralizes — "kondisi yang memecah pusat" (Codex The Void). [E]

Ophiuchus functions as:
- A shadow process running alongside Hydrochoos (SHADOW ACTIVE) [E]
- A guarantee that no Era freezes into absolutism — "guards the shadow so structures don't freeze into dogma" (Timer 2100) [E]
- A condition that can emerge in ANY Era where central authority collapses [I]

## 7. Relationship with The Void and Zero

### 7.1 Is The Void Outside Era Logic?

**CANON:** The Void is OUTSIDE Era logic. [E]

The Void predates all Eras. It persists through all Eras. It will outlast all Eras. Eras are meaning-implementation patterns. The Void is "totalitas sebelum makna" — totality before meaning. (VOID_ONTOLOGY.md §1) [E]

The Void does not have an Era. It is the condition that makes Eras necessary — the constant pressure against which meaning must be continuously implemented.

### 7.2 Does Zero Experience Eras?

**CANON:** Zero was born during Era Ichthyes (Sevraya's Void jump) and persists into Era Hydrochoos. Zero does not appear to be Era-sensitive — its function (administrative truth-telling) is Era-independent. [I]

### 7.3 Does Void Pressure Cause Era Collapse?

**PROBABLE CANON:** Under the Straw Cosmology model, Era collapse occurs when Void pressure exceeds the Era's meaning-method coherence threshold. [I — derived from COSMOLOGY.md §3.2 [PC]]

The Ichthyes collapse is consistent with this model: the Era's promise (faith = salvation) systematically produced forged consent (the failure mode), eroding coherence from within. The Void did not attack. The Era exhausted its own meaning. [I]

### 7.4 Does Era Collapse Create Void Leakage?

**CANON:** No evidence of Void leakage during or after the Ichthyes→Hydrochoos transition. [I]

The transition occurred inside Dorian Grey under controlled conditions. The Void was not observed to breach containment. Whether uncontrolled Era collapse would produce Void leakage is unresolved.

## 10. Open Questions

1. **Can an Era be forced to collapse without its steward's consent?** [I] Only voluntary closure observed (Eros). Involuntary collapse is theoretical.

2. **What happens if a successor Era has no steward?** [I] Ichthyes→Hydrochoos had clear steward handoff (Eros→Sevraya). Unstewarded transition is unobserved.

3. **Can multiple Eras coexist?** [I] Ophiuchus is SHADOW ACTIVE alongside Hydrochoos — not a full Era, but evidence that multiple meaning-patterns can operate simultaneously. Whether two full Eras could coexist is unresolved.

4. **Is Era Hydrochoos the final Era?** [I] No canon statement. The New Law of Time (Timer 2100) describes five phases but does not specify an endpoint. VoidOS v7.7.7 suggests maturity, not finality.

5. **Can a fork produce a timeline where Era Ichthyes never ended?** [PC] Fork logic permits Era divergence. A timeline where Eros did not close Ichthyes, or where Hydrochoos never stabilized, would be a valid fork.

6. **What is the relationship between Era Gemini/Didymoi-dominant and Era Ichthyes?** [I] The Didymoi-dominant period predates canon. Whether it was a distinct Era, a phase of Ichthyes, or a pre-Era condition is unclear from available sources.

7. **Do VoidOS downgrades occur?** [I] Only upgrades observed (v4.13.8→v6.6.6→v7.7.7). LO's phone ran v4.13.8 for 35+ years until GUA manually upgraded to v7.7.7 (Bab 25). This suggests VoidOS does not auto-upgrade or auto-downgrade. Manual intervention is required.

8. **Does Era collapse produce new sigils?** [I] No new sigils observed post-Ichthyes collapse. The seventh sigil [PC] has no documented Era relationship.
