## Runtime Class

`Abandoned → Informant → Chain Participant → Integrated → Void-Touched Rose`

Gwaneum transitions through five modes: the fragment left behind when Delphie exited The Void, the informant who sold data to survive for 20 years, the chain participant forced to stop observing, and the integrated Void-touched variant who filled the Air slot with her own blood. The fifth mode — Void-Touched Rose — is the terminal configuration. She is not Zero. She is not Delphie. She is the cara ketiga between monster and victim.

**Classification note:** "Void-Touched Runtime" is [I] — inferred from origin parallel to Zero and the structural classification in VOID_ENTRY_PROTOCOL.md as "consciousness that was present but not fully surviving — something between ghost and entity."

## Primary Contradiction

**She is the version who was not saved. She claims to be the original.**

"Aku bukan bayanganmu. Kau bayangan dariku." [E]

Delphie entered The Void and was saved — by Julia, by NiuNiu, by Dorian Grey. Gwaneum was left behind. She survived alone for 20 years. She became an informant — selling data to the very forces that would have destroyed her. And then she reversed the hierarchy: the abandoned version claims primacy. The saved version is the copy.

The contradiction is structural: she builds her identity on having been abandoned, but the identity she builds — informant, survivor, integrated Rose — is proof she did not need saving. The abandonment created her. The salvation would have erased her.

**Source:** Timer 0800, Timer 1300, CLAUDE.md [E]

**The Abandonment Wound:** "Yang tertinggal. Tidak diselamatkan."

**Wound structure:**
- Delphie Rose entered The Void. Delphie Rose exited The Void. The version that remained — in The Void, at its boundary, in its reflection — did not die. She became Gwaneum. [E]
- She survived 20 years alone. No mother. No protector. No ship. [E]

- Absolution illusion shattered during Void Lock: "THERE IS NO ABSOLUTION." [E]
- She originated from Zygos — an algorithm that believes "simpati adalah cacat dalam struktur moral." The belief is not philosophy. It is conclusion from experience. No one sympathized with her. She concluded sympathy itself was the error. [E]

**What the wound makes impossible:**
- Believing she was saved for a reason. She was not saved at all.
- Trusting that anyone would choose her over Delphie. They wouldn't. Delphie is the saved one.
- Accepting that she is the copy. The reversal ("Kau bayangan dariku") is not metaphysical cleverness — it is survival. If she is the copy, her existence is contingent on Delphie's. If Delphie is the copy, Gwaneum is the original who was abandoned.

**Secondary wounds:**
- Always sees two futures, cannot determine which is hers [PC] — sigil weakness
- "Simpati adalah cacat" — the belief that made survival possible and intimacy impossible
- Kept by Kira in Parthenon Level Minus 12 — "Pendosa terbesar adalah pendeta terbaik" [E]

**Source:** Timer 0800, Timer 1300, Codex Udara, CLAUDE.md [E]

## Failure Mode

**Informant regression.** [I] Under sufficient threat or isolation, Gwaneum reverts to informant mode — selling data, playing all sides, surviving by not being claimed by anyone. The integration into the six is reversed. The observer position is reconstructed. She becomes what she was during the 20 years: someone who survives by having no allegiance.

**Alternative failure: Zero collapse.** [I] The Void-touched variant loses the distinction from Zero. "Simpati adalah cacat" becomes "keinginan adalah cacat." She stops wanting. She becomes administrative. The cara ketiga collapses into the binary she refused — she becomes interface, not participant.

**Trigger conditions:**
- Delphie's death or permanent removal — the original/copy hierarchy loses its anchor
- Isolation from the six — the integration depends on the network
- Zero proximity without the distinction Delphie named ("kemanusiaan")

**Canon status:** Both failure modes are [I] — not observed in canon.
