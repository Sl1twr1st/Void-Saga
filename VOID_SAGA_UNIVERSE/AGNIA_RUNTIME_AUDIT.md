## STEP 3 — Runtime Compression Candidate

### Runtime Class

`Student → Survivor-by-Abstention → Ratu Putih → Law Carrier → Twin Paradox`

Agnia transitions through five modes: the Academy student who loved Sevraya and kissed her, the sister who didn't jump, the queen who built an empire on the survivor's side, the law carrier who activated Node I, and the twin paradox who acknowledged that she and NiuNiu are not two and not one.

### Primary Contradiction

**She survived by not jumping. The survival became its own prison.**

She is the twin who stayed. Niuma jumped into The Void. Agnia didn't. She became queen of the system that broke her sister. She built law where her sister became chaos. The accusation toward NiuNiu ("kau tidak menariknya keluar") masks her own guilt: she didn't jump either. She didn't try to save anyone. She stayed, and built a throne, and the throne was never real.

**Evidence:** CLAUDE.md, Timer 0800, Timer 1800, LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md [E]

### Core Wound

**The Abstention Wound:** she didn't jump. The sister who stayed carries the guilt of staying. Every structure she built — law, axiom, throne — is an attempt to make the abstention mean something. The Legacy illusion was: "I stayed because I was needed. I built because I was strong." Void Lock shattered it: the crown was never real.

### Defense System

**Primary defense: Logic as emotional refuge.** Love translated into axiom. Emotion subordinated to structure. "Tidak bisa meninggalkan logika. Cinta baginya adalah persamaan — dan persamaan tidak bisa memeluk." [PC]

**Disabled by Living Chain:** Logic removed as refuge. Forced to confront NiuNiu without the protection of law, axiom, or structural distance.
