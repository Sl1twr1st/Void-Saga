## Emergent Behavior

### Constraint Resolution

The constraints resolve to a single permitted pattern: **mutual acknowledgment without hierarchy.**

1. No threat → no combat, no protection reflex
2. Delphie: chain removed distance → must be present, not observe
3. Gwaneum: chain removed observer immunity → must participate
4. Delphie: cannot hate, feels Gwaneum's wound → the abandonment becomes hers
5. Gwaneum: reversal claim silenced but not retracted → "cara ketiga" still sought
6. Paradox constraint: neither can be proven original or copy
7. Resolution: both claims are simultaneously true → hierarchy is the error

**What Delphie says:** Not "You're right." Not "I'm the original." Acknowledgment that salvation was received, not earned. That she did nothing to deserve being the one who was saved. That Gwaneum's 20 years of survival are not less real than her 20 years of being loved.

**What Gwaneum says:** Not "I was wrong." Not "You're the real one." Acknowledgment that humanity — the thing she called "simpati adalah cacat" — is real. Delphie has it. It is not weakness. The reversal claim does not require Delphie's erasure. Both can be true.

**The paradox holds.** Neither admits the other is the original. Both admit the hierarchy was the error — not the claim.
