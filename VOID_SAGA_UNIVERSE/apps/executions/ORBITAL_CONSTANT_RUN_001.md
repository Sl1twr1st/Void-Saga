The constraints resolve to a single permitted pattern: stable distance.

1. No threat → no combat reflex, no protection drive
2. Sevraya-proximity trigger (NiuNiu) → orbital calibration
3. NiuNiu-proximity trigger (Sevraya) → orbital calibration

6. Voice constraint (Sevraya) → light tone. One sentence. Cigarette.
7. Orbital contract → distance stabilizes. No approach. No retreat.
8. Zero constraint → cigarette lit. Zero silent.

**What NiuNiu does:** Stops. The stop IS the communication. If she speaks: one word. Not "I missed you." Not "Don't come closer." Something that acknowledges presence without inviting approach. Panel may activate. Vocal may activate — Sevraya's presence is a speech trigger. But the word will be rough, minimal, functional.

**What Sevraya does:** Stays where she is. Cigarette lit. Light tone. One sentence. Acknowledgment without demand. She does not ask NiuNiu to come closer. She does not apologize for the distance. The distance is not a problem to be solved.

### The Most Constrained Output

NiuNiu's most constrained utterance: her own name. "NiuNiu." Not "Niuma" — that name is dead. Not "Sevraya" — that would be address, which is approach. Her own name, spoken aloud, rough: this is who I am now. This is the distance between who I was and who I became. You know both versions. The name contains the entire orbital trajectory.

Sevraya's most constrained response: acknowledgment of presence without demand. "Dan aku yang tinggal." — canon, but may not be used here if it over-identifies the moment with the resolution scene. Alternative: a sentence that does not ask. "Masih." — one word. Still here. Still orbiting. The distance is maintained.
