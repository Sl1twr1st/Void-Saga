## The win (real — do not erase it)

1. **He dogfooded and found the right thing.** The discovery — *writers think in "what if…", not JSON; the Fork Record is an IR, not the writer's mental model* — is the most important product finding available, and he got it by USING the thing, not theorizing. He applied his own principle (observation precedes interpretation) to the product. The conversational layer above the checker IS the real product.
2. **He narrowed.** Yesterday's vision was a forkable-universe platform with a blockchain ledger. Today he built the narrowest testable slice: one chapter, forkable, working checker, dogfooded with real data. He did NOT build the cathedral. That is the discipline win of the week.

## The relapse (the contra, reborn in a new domain)

He responded to a **one-session** discovery by building a **research institution** around it.

1. **A second engine.** "Question Engine" beside "Invariant Engine" — with its own 6–12 month research question ("how does AI help narrative thinking?"), its own governance doc, its own sprint cadence. A second container. The contra's core warning was: bigger containers to avoid finishing. Now two open-ended research programs compete for one person's time while the novel is unshipped and the extractor (Fase 2) is unbuilt.
2. **Governance for an org of one.** `PRODUCT_RESEARCH_OPERATING_MODEL` — two backlogs, golden rule, "how a hypothesis becomes a feature" — is org-design written before there is a team or even a second participant. The map is bigger than the territory. This is the GUA invariant ("builds systems to contain what can't be said directly") in its most articulate form yet.
3. **N=1, and it's him.** The TTI table, intent variants, session evidence — one session, writer = Jali. Sprint target is 20 interviews; the only writer is him. Same N=1 circularity as contra #3, reproduced in the Question Engine. He is about to build a beautiful dataset of himself interviewing himself.
4. **Confidence theater returns.** The variant table (A: 8 sessions 83%, B: 5 sessions 60%) shows numbers that **do not exist yet**, presented as if measured. Same risk as contra #2.

## The gate insight (the most important line)

Everything bottlenecks on **Fase 2 — automated extraction** (prose/conversation → structured law-claims). The Fork Records are currently **filled by hand from conversation notes** (the discovery doc admits it). The Question Engine's endgame — "NL → Fork Record compiler" — is listed as Sprint 5, which is Fase 2 again. The fork MVP, the question engine, the platform: all of them bottleneck on the same gate. Until it exists, every fork is hand-translated by a human (him). More sprint apparatus does not move the gate. The extractor does. And the extractor waits behind the novel.

## Recommendations

1. **Keep the discovery. Kill the institution.** `FORK_INTERVIEW_RUNTIME_DISCOVERY` is gold — keep. `OPERATING_MODEL` + `SPRINT` are premature governance for N=1 — demote to notes, do not run as process.
2. **One engine, not two.** "Question Engine" is the fork product's UX layer, not a co-equal research program. No separate 6–12 month question.
3. **Next step = 3–5 dogfooding sessions with OTHER people,** not 20 self-interviews. Three second-writers forking Bab 00 conversationally is worth more than the entire sprint apparatus. This is Fase 3 (second author) + the fork test combined. The second writer unlocks BOTH engines.
4. **Genesis is still unsealed.** What people would fork is the 95k-word saga, not one Bab 00. Do not let the fork apparatus substitute for finishing the genesis.
