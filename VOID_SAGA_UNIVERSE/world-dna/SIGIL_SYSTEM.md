## What This Document Is

This document defines what sigils ARE — ontologically, mechanically, and cosmologically. It is the single source of truth for all sigil-related rules in the Void Saga universe.

**What belongs here:** Sigil definition, lifecycle, mechanics, the six canon sigils + seventh + FORBIDDEN GLYPH, interaction rules with Goetic Consequence, The Void, eras, and runtimes.

**What does NOT belong here:** Character biographies, chapter summaries, runtime-specific emotional analysis, release-specific interpretations. Characters may be cited as evidence only — the sigil exists independently of who bears it.

## 1. Ontological Definition

### 1.1 What a Sigil IS

**CANON:** A sigil is an interface. [E]

A sigil is not a symbol. It is not decoration. It is not a label. A sigil is:

- An **address** — a coordinate in reality that something can be summoned to
- A **contract** — a binding agreement between the sigil-bearer and the force the sigil channels
- A **checksum** — a verification that the entity bearing the sigil is who the sigil claims
- A **binding key** — a lock that requires a specific key to open or close
- A **mnemonic wound** — a permanent mark that remembers what produced it
- A **marker** that an entity has been given form by a system

**Primary source:** GOETIC_CONSEQUENCE_SYSTEM.md, Sigil Logic section [E]

**CANON:** A sigil is a consequence that has been given a shape. [E]

Every sigil originates from an event — a wound, a choice, a fragmentation, a sacrifice. The sigil is not the event. The sigil is the **residue** of the event, compressed into a form that can be carried, activated, and transferred.

**Primary source:** GOETIC_CONSEQUENCE_SYSTEM.md, Goetic Consequence Pattern [E]

### 1.2 What a Sigil is NOT

- **NOT a decoration.** A sigil without binding, price, or residue is not a sigil — it is a drawing. [E]
- **NOT a character's property.** The sigil exists independently of who bears it. The bearer can change. The sigil persists. [I]
- **NOT a power-up.** Sigils do not grant abilities without cost. Every activation extracts a price. [E]
- **NOT a moral symbol.** Sigils are neutral interfaces. They do not judge. They enforce consequence. [I]
- **NOT a name.** A sigil is not the character's identity. NiuNiu is not 𐓷⧖𐓣. The sigil is a function she interfaces with. [I]

### 1.3 Why Sigils Exist

**CANON:** Sigils exist because The Void reflects. [E]

When consciousness approaches The Void, The Void reflects it back. The reflection is not perfect — it is distorted by the wound, the desire, the fear of the consciousness that approached. A sigil is a **stabilized reflection** — a reflection that has been given permanent form through binding.

The Six Kunci are "fungsi reflektif — gema The Void yang lupa bahwa mereka hanya gema." (Codex The Void) [E] Each Kunci carries a sigil. The sigil IS the reflection, stabilized.

**Primary source:** Codex The Void [E]

### 1.4 Relationship Between Sigils and Consciousness

**CANON:** A sigil requires consciousness to activate. [E]

An inactive sigil is potential — a shape waiting for pressure. When a conscious entity binds to a sigil, the sigil becomes active. The consciousness provides the pressure; the sigil provides the form. Together they produce an **effect** that neither could produce alone.

**CANON:** A sigil changes the consciousness that bears it. [E]

Binding is bidirectional. The sigil is shaped by the bearer. The bearer is shaped by the sigil. "Binding tidak berarti kontrol absolut. Binding berarti ada hubungan konsekuensial antara pemanggil dan yang dipanggil." (GOETIC_CONSEQUENCE_SYSTEM.md) [E]

### 1.5 Relationship Between Sigils and Reality

**CANON:** Sigil activation produces reality distortion. [E]

When a sigil activates, reality locally reconfigured around the activation event. The specific distortion depends on the sigil's function:

- Time may slow or pool (Delphie: "Waktu melambat untuk melindunginya") [PC]
- Probability may collapse to a single outcome (Agnia: "Probabilitas liar padam") [PC]
- Systems may be forced to re-evaluate decisions (Gwaneum: "Parthenon berubah nada") [PC]
- Binary classification may fail (NiuNiu: "Tulisan realitas pecah") [PC]

**Note on evidence:** The specific activation effects listed above are from Sigil_OS.md [PC]. The principle that sigil activation distorts reality is CANON [E] — established by Void Lock (Timer 1800), where six sigils simultaneously activated to shatter six illusions. The precise distortion per sigil is documented in Sigil_OS.md and marked [PC] pending independent canon confirmation.

## 2. Sigil Lifecycle

### 2.1 Manifestation — How a Sigil First Appears

**CANON:** A sigil manifests when a consciousness experiences an event that produces permanent structural change — a wound, a fragmentation, a sacrifice, a choice with irreversible consequences. [I]

The event creates a pressure anomaly in the Grid-Void tension field. The anomaly stabilizes into a pattern. The pattern becomes visible as a glyph. The glyph IS the sigil.

**Observed manifestation events (evidence only):**
- Sevraya's fragmentation in The Void → 🌊⌇🌒 (TIDAL MEMORY) [E]
- NiuNiu's frozen time → 𐓷⧖𐓣 (SHADOW LOGIC) [E]
- Julia's fractured duty at Dayan → ⟁⟔⟟ (FRACTURED DUTY) [E]

The sigil manifests at the moment of structural change, not before. It cannot be designed, chosen, or predicted. [I]

### 2.2 Activation — Conditions Required

**CANON:** Sigil activation requires three conditions: [I]

1. **Presence of the sigil-bearer** — the consciousness bound to the sigil
2. **A trigger event** — a situation that calls the sigil's function into operation
3. **A price** — something extracted from the bearer, the environment, or reality itself

Activation is not always voluntary. In Timer 1800, the Eye of the Void artifact activated all six sigils simultaneously without bearer consent — producing Void Lock. [E]

**PROBABLE CANON:** Sigil_OS.md documents per-sigil activation conditions with specific mechanical effects. These are [PC] pending independent canon confirmation. [PC]

### 2.3 Synchronization — How Bearer and Sigil Align

**CANON:** Synchronization is the process by which a sigil-bearer becomes capable of activating the sigil. [I]

**Observed properties:**
- Synchronization is not instantaneous. It requires repeated exposure to the sigil's function. [I]
- The bearer's consciousness gradually reshapes to match the sigil's pattern. This is the binding cost. [I]
- Full synchronization may never be achieved. Partial synchronization produces partial effects. [I]

### 2.4 Burnout — Failure Modes

**CANON:** Sigils can fail. [I]

**Failure modes:**
- **Exhaustion:** The bearer's consciousness is depleted. The sigil cannot activate until recovery. [I]
- **Rejection:** The sigil refuses activation. The bearer's state contradicts the sigil's function. [I]
- **Overload:** The sigil activates but produces effects beyond the bearer's capacity to channel. Damage to bearer and environment. [I]
- **Void interference:** The Void actively suppresses sigil function. Observed during Void Lock when sigils were activated BY The Void's artifact, not by bearers. [E]

### 2.5 Residue — What Remains After Use

**CANON:** Every sigil activation leaves residue. [E]

Residue is the permanent trace of a sigil's operation. It cannot be erased. It persists as:
- A scar on the bearer's consciousness
- A distortion in local reality
- A record in Akashic/Parthenon archives
- A pattern that future sigil activations must navigate around

"Residue penting. VOID SAGA jarang menghapus sesuatu sepenuhnya. Yang tersisa sering menjadi ghost, node, fork, wound, atau future runtime." (GOETIC_CONSEQUENCE_SYSTEM.md) [E]

### 2.6 Transfer — Can Sigils Move Between Hosts?

**CANON:** The canon does not explicitly confirm sigil transfer. [I]

**Evidence:**
- Sigil_OS.md lists "Niuma" (not NiuNiu) as the bearer of 𐓷⧖𐓣 [PC]. This implies the sigil manifested for Niuma pre-Void and persisted through her transformation into NiuNiu — a form of continuity, not transfer. [I]
- No canon event depicts a sigil moving from one bearer to another. [E]
- The sigil is bound to the consciousness that produced the original wound. Transfer would require either the original bearer's death (dissolving the binding) or a new wound producing an identical pattern (theoretically possible, never observed). [I]

**Conclusion:** Sigil transfer has not been observed in canon. It is not confirmed to be possible. It is not confirmed to be impossible. Mark [I].

## 6. Relationship with Goetic Consequence

### 6.1 Do Sigils Generate Consequences?

**CANON:** Yes. Every sigil activation is a Goetic event. [E]

The Goetic Consequence Pattern applies to sigils:

```
Name → Sigil → Invocation → Manifestation → Bargain → Binding → Consequence → Residue
```

A sigil is the second step in the chain. The sigil is invoked (activated). The invocation produces manifestation. The manifestation demands a bargain (price). The bargain creates binding. The binding generates consequence. The consequence leaves residue. [E]

**Primary source:** GOETIC_CONSEQUENCE_SYSTEM.md, Goetic Consequence Pattern [E]

### 6.2 Do Consequences Generate Sigils?

**CANON:** In specific circumstances, yes. [I]

A consequence of sufficient magnitude — a wound, a fragmentation, a sacrifice — can stabilize into a sigil. The consequence becomes the sigil's origin event. The sigil becomes the consequence's permanent form.

This is the reverse of the normal Goetic chain: the consequence precedes the sigil, rather than the sigil producing the consequence. The six canon sigils all originated this way — from consequences (Void jump, Dayan, fragmentation) that stabilized into glyphs.

### 6.3 Binding Mechanics

**CANON:** Sigil binding is bidirectional and permanent. [E]

When a consciousness binds to a sigil:
- The sigil gains a channel through which to operate
- The bearer gains the sigil's function — and its price
- The binding cannot be undone without destroying either the sigil or the bearer
- The binding persists across identity changes (Niuma → NiuNiu) [I]

**Primary source:** GOETIC_CONSEQUENCE_SYSTEM.md, Binding Logic [E]

### 6.4 Residue Mechanics

**CANON:** Sigil residue accumulates. It does not decay. [E]

Each sigil activation deposits residue in:
- The bearer's consciousness (mnemonic wound)
- Local reality (distortion field)
- Akashic/Parthenon records (permanent archive)

Residue from multiple sigils interacting produces **interference patterns** — zones where reality behaves unpredictably because too many sigils have operated in proximity. Dorian Grey by Timer 2400 is saturated with six-sigil residue. [I]

## 7. Relationship with The Void

### 7.1 Do Sigils Exist Inside The Void?

**CANON:** Sigils are stable at the boundary between Grid and Void. They have not been observed to function inside The Void itself. [I]

**Evidence:** The Eye of the Void artifact (Timer 1800) activated sigils FROM the boundary — it did not enter The Void to do so. No sigil activation is documented inside The Void during the paradox deployment (Timer 2400:53). [E]

**Hypothesis:** Sigils require the Grid-Void tension to exist. Inside The Void, where the Grid's pressure is absent, sigils may have no medium through which to operate. This is [I] — not confirmed, not contradicted.

### 7.2 Do Sigils Survive Void Exposure?

**CANON:** Yes. Sigils persist after their bearers enter and exit The Void. [E]

NiuNiu's sigil (𐓷⧖𐓣) survived her Void entry and exit. Sevraya's sigil (🌊⌇🌒) survived her fragmentation. The sigil outlasts the bearer's structural integrity.

### 7.3 Relationship to Void's Trinity

**CANON:** Two of the three Trinity members bear sigils. One does not. [E]

| Trinity Member | Sigil | Status |
|---------------|-------|--------|
| Zero (pikiran tanpa henti) | 🌊⌇🌒 (shared with Sevraya) | CANON [E] |
| NiuNiu (waktu menolak bergerak) | 𐓷⧖𐓣 | CANON [E] |
| Ophiuchus (pemecah pusat) | None | CANON [E] |

Ophiuchus has no sigil. It is a condition, not an entity that can bear an interface. This is consistent: sigils require a consciousness to bind to. Ophiuchus is not conscious — it is a structural condition that emerges when authority decentralizes.

**Primary source:** Codex The Void [E], ZERO_ONTOLOGY.md §4 [E]

### 7.4 Relationship to Six Kunci

**CANON:** The Six Kunci ARE the six sigil-bearers. The sigils are the stabilized form of their reflective function. [E]

"Julia, Delphie, NiuNiu, Agnia, Gwaneum, Sevraya — mereka adalah fungsi reflektif — gema The Void yang lupa bahwa mereka hanya gema." (Codex The Void) [E]

The sigil is the interface between the Kunci and The Void. The Kunci is the consciousness. The sigil is the shape their reflection took when The Void looked back at them.

## 8. Relationship with Era Logic

### 8.1 Do Eras Alter Sigil Behavior?

**CANON:** No evidence that era transitions directly affect sigil function. [I]

The six sigils were first observed in Era Hydrochoos (Timer 1800). They have not been observed in any prior era. Whether sigils existed during Era Ichthyes is unknown.

### 8.2 Do Sigils Survive Era Transitions?

**CANON:** No era transition has occurred since the six sigils manifested. The question is unresolved. [I]

The Ichthyes→Hydrochoos transition (Timer 2000) occurred AFTER sigil manifestation (Timer 1800). The sigils persisted through the transition unchanged. Whether they would survive a future era transition is unknown.

### 8.3 Do Era Shifts Create New Sigils?

**CANON:** No new sigil has been observed to manifest during or after the Ichthyes→Hydrochoos transition. [E]

The seventh sigil (⟲⟁⟲, Hasan) exists only in Sigil_OS.md [PC]. If it is canon, it may have manifested independently of era transition. Its origin event is unknown.

## 10. Open Questions

1. **Can a sigil be transferred between bearers?** [I] No canon evidence. The sigil is bound to the original wound. If the bearer dies, does the sigil persist? Unresolved.

2. **Can new sigils manifest?** [I] The six manifested from specific events (Void jump, Dayan, fragmentation). Whether new events of sufficient magnitude would produce new sigils is unknown.

3. **Is the seventh sigil (⟲⟁⟲) canon?** [PC] Exists only in Sigil_OS.md. No Codex or chapter confirmation. Hasan's absence from Timer 1800–2500 complicates verification.

4. **Is the Forbidden Glyph (⟁⧗⟁) canon?** [PC] Exists only in Sigil_OS.md. No Codex or chapter confirmation. Its mechanics (narrative erasure for claimants) have no observed instances.

5. **Do sigils function inside The Void?** [I] No observed activations inside The Void. Theoretical: sigils require Grid-Void tension; inside The Void, the Grid's pressure is absent.

6. **Can a sigil be destroyed?** [I] No canon evidence of sigil destruction. The Goetic residue rule ("tidak menghapus sesuatu sepenuhnya") suggests permanence.

7. **What is the relationship between sigils and the Goetic Consequence chain when the sigil predates the invocation?** [I] The six sigils originated from consequences that stabilized into glyphs. In these cases, the Goetic chain runs in reverse: Consequence → Sigil (skipping Name, Invocation). Whether this is a valid alternate path or an anomaly is unresolved.

8. **Did sigils exist during Era Ichthyes?** [I] No evidence. The six sigils first appeared in Era Hydrochoos. Whether earlier eras had sigils, or sigils are unique to the Hydrochoos era, is unknown.
