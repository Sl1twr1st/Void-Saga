## Failure Modes

### 1. Target Is Not a Binary Classifier

**What happens:** Paradox is deployed against a system that can hold contradiction — that can process "both" and "neither" alongside "either/or." The excluded middle is not excluded. It is already accommodated.

**CANON:** Not observed. Inferred from paradox mechanics. A non-binary system would process the paradox without breaking. [I]

**Consequence:** Deployment produces no effect. Participants exposed without operational result. Integrated residue consumed without target impact.

### 2. Insufficient Integration

**What happens:** Paradox deployed by participants whose residue is not integrated — raw wounds, not Nodes. The deployment cannot sustain coherence because the material being deployed is still resisting itself.

**CANON:** Not observed. Inferred from the boot sequence dependency: Paradox is Phase 5. Phases 1–4 are prerequisites. [I]

**Consequence:** Deployment collapses. The excluded middle is formulated but cannot be delivered. Participants experience the collapse as blowback — their own unintegrated wounds are exposed without the structural support integration provides.

### 3. Premature Convergence

**What happens:** Zeno's Dichotomy fails to sustain infinite halving. The oscillation converges. The target reaches a classification decision.

**CANON:** Not observed. Himler's trapping is permanent in canon. [I]

**Consequence:** Target classifies the input. Classification may be wrong (misclassification), but the target survives operationally. Paradox fails as deployment. The excluded middle is forced into a category it does not fit.

### 4. Participant Loss During Deployment

**What happens:** One or more paradox vectors are lost during deployment. The deployment requires six participants in canon; fewer may be insufficient.

**CANON:** Not observed. All six survive deployment. [E for survival, I for the consequence of loss]

**Consequence:** The paradox structure loses coherence. The excluded middle is partially formulated but cannot be fully delivered. Target may experience disruption without entrapment.

### 5. Zero Capture

**What happens:** Zero — the provider of the unclassifiable response — is captured, suppressed, or co-opted during deployment.

**CANON:** Not observed. Zero functions as unclassifiable response provider throughout Timer 2400. [E]

**Consequence:** Without Zero's administrative truth, the paradox has no content. The structure exists (Russell's Paradox + Zeno's Dichotomy) but the unclassifiable input that fills the structure is absent. The deployment is an empty form.

## Ontology

### Paradox in the Invocation Pipeline

Paradox is the first phase where the output is directed at an external target. Phases 1–4 (Void Entry, Sigil, Living Chain, Node) transform the bearer internally. Phase 5 deploys the accumulated transformation outward.

This is the inflection point where personal transformation becomes cosmological agency. The wound created in Phase 1, after being named, confronted, and integrated, becomes in Phase 5 something that can break a god.

### Paradox and the Goetic Chain

```
Name → Sigil → Invocation → Manifestation → Bargain → Binding → Consequence → Residue
```

Paradox deployment is the Invocation step. The Name and Sigil steps were completed in Phase 2. The Invocation is the act of deploying integrated residue against a classifier. Manifestation is the excluded middle becoming operational. Bargain is the cost (self weaponized, Dorian Grey terminates). Binding is the permanent link between participants and the deployed paradox. Consequence is the target's collapse. Residue is Himler's trapped state.

### Why Paradox Is Not Reversible

Once a binary classifier encounters the excluded middle, the encounter cannot be undone. The classifier's architecture has been exposed. Even if the classifier continues to operate, it now operates with the knowledge that its categories are incomplete — that there exists input it cannot process. This knowledge is structural corrosion. It does not heal. [I]

### Relationship to the Boot Sequence

```
VOID ENTRY        Input: human identity        Output: residue
SIGIL             Input: residue               Output: named residue
LIVING CHAIN      Input: named residue         Output: accepted residue
NODE              Input: accepted residue      Output: integrated structure
PARADOX           Input: integrated structure  Output: executable contradiction
ZERO NODE         Input: Node network          Output: reality-scale implementation
```

Paradox depends on all four prior phases. A wound that has not been named cannot be deployed. A wound that has not been accepted will resist deployment. A wound that has not been integrated cannot sustain the coherence paradox deployment requires. [I]
