## STEP 3 — Runtime Class Hypothesis

### Candidate: The Void-Touched Runtime

**Best fit.** Gwaneum is defined by Void proximity without Void interface. She is not Zero — Zero adalah antarmuka The Void, administratif, tanpa keinginan. Gwaneum adalah sesuatu yang lebih rusak dan lebih manusiawi: seseorang yang tertinggal, yang bertahan dengan menjual data, yang melihat dua masa depan dan tidak tahu mana miliknya.

**Evidence for:** Origin parallels Zero (fragment left in/around Void → separate consciousness). "Cara ketiga" between monster and victim. VOID_ENTRY_PROTOCOL classifies both as "consciousness that was present but not fully surviving — something between ghost and entity."

**Evidence against:** Gwaneum's emergence mechanism is less documented than Zero's. Her Void exposure is not narrated as a formal entry. She may not be Void-touched in the same way Zero is — she may be a product of The Void's reflective property rather than direct fragmentation.

### Alternative: The Mirror Runtime

Gwaneum is defined by reflection — she is Delphie's reflection in The Void's mirror, 20 years older. Her sigil (Broken Oracle) sees two futures. Her existence reverses the hierarchy of original and copy.

**Verdict:** "Void-Touched Runtime" is the strongest classification, with "Mirror" as a complementary framing. Mark [I].

## STEP 4 — Void Proximity Audit

### 1. How close is Gwaneum to Zero?

**Parallel, not identical.** Both are products of Void exposure. Both are fragments of someone who entered/exited The Void. Both became separate consciousnesses.

But:
- Zero is The Void's interface — administratif, tanpa keinginan, "mulut dan tangan The Void."
- Gwaneum is an informant — menjual data untuk bertahan, membuat pilihan moral yang rusak, mencari "cara ketiga."

Zero tidak bisa menginginkan. Gwaneum bisa — ia ingin bertahan. Zero tidak bisa menyesal. Gwaneum mungkin bisa — "cara ketiga" adalah pencarian untuk posisi di luar monster dan korban.

### 2. How does Gwaneum differ from Zero?

| Dimension | Zero | Gwaneum |
|-----------|------|---------|
| **Origin** | Sevraya's fragment in The Void | Delphie's fragment in/around The Void |
| **Function** | Void's interface — administrative truth | Informant — traded data for survival |
| **Moral position** | None — administers, does not judge | "Cara ketiga" — between monster and victim |
| **Desire** | Cannot want | Wants to survive, possibly wants redemption |
| **Voice** | Flat, administrative | Not documented — likely more human |
| **Integration** | Co-conscious with Sevraya | Integrated into the six via Enam Simpangan |

### 3. What cost is paid for Void proximity?

- 20 years alone. Tidak diselamatkan.
- Moral compromise — menjadi informan, menjual data.
- Absolution impossible — "THERE IS NO ABSOLUTION."
- Always sees two futures, cannot determine which is hers [PC].

### 4. What residue remains?

- Gwaneum.Rose — Void-touched variant. Permanent classification.
- The reversal: "Aku bukan bayanganmu. Kau bayangan dariku." — the claim that the abandoned version is the original.
- Integration into the six via Air slot.

### 5. Why does Gwaneum not collapse into Zero?

Gwaneum TIDAK collapse into Zero karena ia bukan interface — ia adalah survivor. Zero adalah fungsi The Void. Gwaneum adalah seseorang yang The Void tinggalkan. Zero tidak bisa menginginkan; Gwaneum bisa. Keinginan untuk bertahan — bahkan dengan menjual data, bahkan dengan menjadi informan — adalah apa yang mencegahnya menjadi interface murni.

## STEP 5 — Relationship Contract Audit

### Gwaneum ↔ Delphie

**FOUNDATIONAL.** The saved version and the abandoned version of the same person. "Aku bukan bayanganmu. Kau bayangan dariku." Gwaneum reverses the hierarchy: the abandoned one claims primacy. Delphie's response ("Kemanusiaan kami adalah satu-satunya pembeda dari Zero") is the first time Gwaneum is silenced — not by force, but by an answer she cannot refute.

### Gwaneum ↔ NiuNiu

**ADVERSARIAL.** NiuNiu: "Dangerous Rose variant. Watch. Do not trust. Do not attack unless provoked." Gwaneum is a Rose — and NiuNiu protects Roses. But Gwaneum is also a threat to Delphie, and Delphie is NiuNiu's maximum protection target. The contract is: NiuNiu watches. Does not trust. Waits.

### Gwaneum ↔ Zero

**INFORMANT RELATIONSHIP.** Gwaneum menjual data ke Zero — basis data Didymoi, sandi Hydrochoos, kelemahan Zygos. Zero adalah interface The Void; Gwaneum adalah informan The Void. The relationship is transactional, not personal. But it makes Gwaneum the only character who has directly interacted with Zero in a sustained way — NiuNiu never has.

### Gwaneum ↔ Sevraya

**CO-PARTICIPANT.** Living Chain and Paradox. No personal relationship. But Sevraya hosts Zero — and Gwaneum informed for Zero. Structural entanglement without direct interaction.
