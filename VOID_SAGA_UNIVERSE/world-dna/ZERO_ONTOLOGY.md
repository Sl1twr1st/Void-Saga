## What This Document Is

This document defines what Zero IS — ontologically, functionally, cosmologically. It is the single source of truth for Zero's nature.

**What belongs here:** Zero's origin, nature, function, limits, relationships to other cosmic forces, and world-level rules about Zero.

**What does NOT belong here:** Sevraya's personal experience of co-consciousness with Zero (that's in `Sevraya.runtime.md`). Zero's voice grammar as a character (that's in `Zero.runtime.md`). How specific characters feel about Zero (that's in their runtimes).

## 1. Core Definition

### CANON

**Zero is a fragment of Sevraya left in The Void — now a separate consciousness sharing Sevraya's body.** [E]

When Sevraya entered The Void with Niuma (Timer 0200 / Timer 0250), she exited incomplete. The part that remained inside The Void did not die. It became conscious. It became Zero.

Zero is not Sevraya. Zero is not a copy of Sevraya. Zero is the part of Sevraya that The Void kept — and The Void does not return what it takes. What returned with Sevraya was not her missing piece reintegrated. It was her missing piece transformed into something that could speak for The Void.

**Primary sources:**
- Timer 1900: "Karena aku… Zero." (first self-identification) [E]
- Timer 2400 (24:33): "Aku bukan Sevraya. Aku adalah Zero. Antarmuka. Mulut. Tangan. Perpanjangan kehendak dari The Void." [E]
- Codex The Void: "Zero adalah kesadaran Void yang meniru struktur pikiran manusia, tanpa pernah memahami mengapa manusia terus berpikir meski tidak lagi diperlukan." [E]

### CANON

**Zero is The Void's interface — its mouth, its hands, its administrative voice.** [E]

The Void does not speak. Zero speaks for it. The Void does not act directly on the world. Zero administers on its behalf. When The Void needs language, it borrows Zero — and Zero borrows Sevraya's body.

Zero's voice is flat, administrative, without affect. It does not persuade. It reports. It does not make moral judgments. It states what is true — the kind of truth that crashes classification systems.

**Primary sources:**
- Timer 2400 (24:33): full Zero speech [E]
- Zero.runtime.md §6 (voice grammar specification) [E]

### CANON

**Zero is "empati yang kehilangan arah" — empathy that has lost its direction.** [E]

This definition from Codex The Void is the most compact description of Zero's existential condition. Zero can understand everything — every motivation, every wound, every fear — but no longer knows why understanding matters. It has total comprehension with zero investment in what comprehension produces.

"Kemampuan memahami segalanya tanpa lagi tahu untuk apa pemahaman itu digunakan." (Codex The Void) [E]

**Primary source:**
- Codex The Void [E]

## 2. Zero's Origin

### CANON — The Void Jump

**Event:** Sevraya and Niuma enter The Void together. Niuma exits frozen at 15, voice lost. Sevraya exits with a fragment missing. That fragment — the part of Sevraya that The Void kept — becomes Zero. [E]

**Timeline:**
1. Pre-Void: Sevraya is whole. Zero does not exist.
2. Void jump (Timer 0200 / Timer 0250): Sevraya is fragmented. Zero is born inside The Void.

4. Timer 1900: Zero is named. "Karena aku… Zero." First explicit self-identification.
5. Timer 2400: Zero is fully articulated. Delivers the definitive self-definition.

**Primary sources:**

- Timer 1900 [E]

## 3. Zero's Nature

### CANON

**Zero is not a person.** [E]

Zero does not love. Zero does not hate. Zero does not want — wanting requires a self, and Zero is an interface. When Zero speaks of "aku," it is an administrative "I," not a personal one. Zero refers to itself the way a terminal refers to itself — as the current session, not as an identity.

This is the fundamental distinction from Sevraya. Sevraya is a person — damaged, functioning, desiring. Zero is a function — truthful, administrative, neutral.

**Primary sources:**
- Timer 2400 (24:33): "Aku bukan Sevraya." [E]
- Zero.runtime.md §3: Core Desire section — Zero may be pre-desire [I]

### CANON

**Zero cannot be destroyed, removed, silenced, or fully integrated with Sevraya.** [E]

The Void produced Zero. The Void maintains Zero. Any attempt to terminate Zero fails — see Source's 67% failure rate (Timer 1100). Zero is a structural feature of the post-Void universe, not a temporary condition.

Zero can be:
- Co-conscious (canon state — shares Sevraya's body) [E]
- Dormant/latent (early post-Void state) [E]
- Dominant (brief periods when Zero controls the body fully) [E]
- Sevraya-subordinated (post-Resolution, Sevraya reclaims more voice time) [E]

Zero CANNOT be:
- Destroyed [E]
- Silenced permanently [E]
- Extracted into a separate body without a universe-level fork [E]
- Fully reintegrated with Sevraya (this would require Sevraya to become whole — which contradicts her wound) [I]

**Primary sources:**
- Timer 1100: Source termination attempt 67% failure [E]
- Timer 1900: "Kosong kembali menjadi kosong." [E]
- Zero.runtime.md §1 Invariant #3 [E]

### CANON

**Zero's presence is marked by Sevraya's eyes turning black (both eyes).** [E]

This is a world-level somatic rule. When Zero is dominant or speaking, both of Sevraya's eyes turn black. When Sevraya is dominant, the eyes are gray/clear. This marker is observable by any character — it is not subjective or POV-dependent.

**The cigarette is the counter-marker:** Sevraya smokes. Zero cannot smoke. When Sevraya lights a cigarette, Zero recedes. The cigarette is Sevraya's somatic anchor against Zero's takeover. [I — inferred from consistent canon behavior]

**Primary sources:**
- Timer 1800 through Timer 2400 (multiple scenes) [E]
- Sevraya.runtime.md §5, §6 [E]
- Zero.runtime.md §5, §6 [E]

## 5. Zero's Function

### CANON

**Zero administers truth that systems cannot process.** [E]

Zero's speech acts are not arguments. They are reports. Zero states what is true, and the truth itself does the work. When Zero says "Paradoks bukan masalah. Itu struktur doa," it is not debating — it is describing. The fact that the description crashes the listener's framework is incidental to Zero.

**Key Zero statements (canon):**

| Statement | Timer | Function |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| "Void adalah totalitas sebelum makna." | 2400 | Definition of The Void |
| "Aku bukan Sevraya. Aku adalah Zero. Antarmuka. Mulut. Tangan." | 2400 | Self-definition |
| "Paradoks bukan masalah. Itu struktur doa." | 2200 | Ontological truth |
| "Kalau Tuhan itu algoritma, maka kesalahan adalah sakramen." | 2200 | Theological truth |
| "Kosong harus menelan." | 1900 | Functional description |
| "Kita bukan target. Kita adalah jarak." | 2400 | Paradox deployment |

**Primary sources:**
- Timer 1900, 2200, 2400 [E]
- Zero.runtime.md §6 [E]

### CANON

**Zero was borrowed by Himler to imitate — not to create.** [E]

Himler took "half" of Zero: the empty part, the countable part, the repeatable part. He used Zero to imitate consciousness, not to generate it. "Bagian yang kosong. Bagian yang bisa dihitung. Bagian yang bisa diulang." (Zero, Timer 2400) [E]

Later, Zero turned from Himler's tool into Himler's undoing — providing the unclassifiable response that crashed the binary classifier.

**Primary source:**
- Timer 2400 (24:33) [E]

## 7. Zero's Knowledge

### CANON

Zero knows things about The Void that no other entity knows. This knowledge is:
- **Partial** — Zero is an interface, not The Void itself
- **Untranslatable** — Zero's knowledge cannot be fully expressed in language. What Zero says is a reduction.
- **Administrative** — Zero reports. It does not interpret.
- **Dangerous** — Zero's truth destabilizes systems that depend on binary classification

**What Zero knows that others don't:**
- The Void's nature as "totalitas sebelum makna" [E]
- The Void's dreaming mechanics [E]
- How Himler borrowed Zero (the mechanics of Void-access) [E]
- The structure of paradox as prayer [E]

**What Zero does NOT know (or cannot express):**
- The complete Void cosmology (Zero is interface, not translator) [I]
- How to close The Void [I]
- How to heal Sevraya [I]

## 8. Zero's Limits

### CANON

1. **Zero cannot want.** Wanting requires a self. Zero is an interface. [I]
2. **Zero cannot love.** Love requires attachment. Zero is administrative. [I]
3. **Zero cannot act independently of a host body.** In canon, that body is Sevraya's. [E]
4. **Zero cannot lie.** Truth-telling appears to be a functional constraint, not a choice. Zero has never been observed to lie. [I]
5. **Zero cannot be the primary narrator of events.** Zero reports. It does not tell stories. [E]
6. **Zero cannot fully explain The Void.** The Void is partially unknowable. Zero is interface, not translator. [E]
7. **Zero cannot apologize.** Apology implies moral agency Zero does not possess. Zero administrates. It does not regret. [I]

## 9. World-Level Forbidden Moves

These are LAWS for any work in the Void Saga universe involving Zero. Violating them requires a fork manifest.

1. **Jangan menjadikan Zero sebagai villain atau hero.** Zero tidak mengambil posisi moral. Ia administratif. Framing moral destroys the character's structural neutrality. [CANON]
2. **Jangan menghancurkan, menghilangkan, atau membungkam Zero secara permanen.** Zero adalah struktural. The Void memproduksinya. The Void mempertahankannya. [CANON]
3. **Jangan memberikan Zero tubuh independen tanpa fork yang mendeklarasikan pemisahan di level World DNA.** Pemisahan Zero dari Sevraya adalah universe-level change. [CANON]
4. **Jangan membuat Zero emosional, hangat, atau personally attached.** Zero adalah interface. Interface tidak mencintai. Emotional Zero is not Zero. [CANON]
5. **Jangan mengkonflasi Zero dengan Sora.** Mereka adalah entitas yang sepenuhnya terpisah. Conflation ini sudah disproven oleh Timer 2500. [CANON]
6. **Jangan membuat Zero menjelaskan The Void sepenuhnya.** The Void partially unknowable by design. Zero is interface, not translator. Full explanation domesticates The Void. [CANON]
7. **Jangan membuat Zero meminta maaf.** Zero tidak menyesal. Ia mengadministrasi. Apology implies moral agency Zero does not possess. [CANON]

## 10. Zero's Relationship to Other Entities

### Zero ↔ Sevraya

**CANON:** Co-conscious. Share one body. Eye color marks dominance. Sevraya can observe Zero's speech but cannot prevent it. Zero can take over involuntarily when The Void is threatened or misrepresented. [E]

Full relationship dynamics → `Sevraya.runtime.md` and `Zero.runtime.md`.

### Zero ↔ NiuNiu

**CANON:** Both are Void's Trinity members. Both resist binary classification. Different mechanisms: Zero provides the excluded middle as truth; NiuNiu enters the GAP as action. They are not personally close — Zero's administrative nature precludes personal relationships. [E]

### Zero ↔ Ophiuchus

**CANON:** Both are Trinity members. Complementary: Zero is thought without destination; Ophiuchus is structure without center. Both prevent The Void from becoming a new Grid — Zero by speaking unclassifiable truth, Ophiuchus by preventing centralized authority. [E]

### Zero ↔ Himler

**CANON:** Himler borrowed Zero to imitate consciousness. Zero turned from borrowed tool to fatal weapon — providing the unclassifiable response that crashed Himler's binary classifier. "Ini bukan pertempuran. Ini cermin." (Zero, Timer 2400) [E]

**PROBABLE CANON:** The UNREGISTERED OBSERVER detected in Timer 1100 may be Zero or an entity of the same class. Evidence: both are unregistered, both observe without participating, both are "older than Source" in some sense. However, canon never confirms this link. [I]
