## Primary Contradiction

**She knows what children should not know. Knowledge without feeling is incomplete. She cannot unknow.**

Delphie understands systems — political, mechanical, cosmological — before she finishes understanding herself. Her VOID MANUSCRIPT IX proposed decentralized breathing. Her Remisi Resonansi framework decentralized authority across 12 clans. Her sigil reads buried emotions of systems. She is structurally incapable of not seeing how things work.

Of becoming what NiuNiu became — the protector who cannot stop protecting, the weapon who survived the person who loved. Delphie fears her capacity to understand will become her only self, the way NiuNiu's capacity to protect became hers.

```

// COST: She understands everything. She feels nothing.
```

**Trigger conditions:**
- Network saturated with unprocessed wounds (high-residue environment)
- Extended isolation from Merge connections (loses the triadic anchor)
- NiuNiu unavailable — the mirror who reflects her fear back to her is absent

**Canon status:** Absorption collapse is [I] — inferred from sigil weakness [PC] and structural position as honesty carrier. Strategic retreat is [I] — inferred from defense pattern and the possibility of regression.
