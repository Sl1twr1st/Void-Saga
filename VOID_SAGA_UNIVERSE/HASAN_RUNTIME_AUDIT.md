## STEP 2 — Evidence Clusters

### Identity

Hasan Al Hul. Kapten Akashic Records — kapal perompak Leviathan-class, 20km. Bukan bajak laut konvensional: operasi profesional, terstruktur, lampu hangat keemasan di dalam kapal yang tampak rusak di luar. Kerusakan sebagai identitas. Berkulit gelap, jenggot rapi, mata tajam. Senyum terlalu santai untuk situasi berbahaya. Bicara seperti sistem tapi dengan humor — theater yang dihitung, bukan kosong.

Pilot Dorian Grey via Didymoi seal (chip Delta 4 dari NiuNiu). Suami Sevraya — dikonfirmasi Kira di Timer 0800. Koneksi ke Sora — "Sora adalah katalis. Untuk banyak hal. Termasuk Delphie." Berada di Sektor 13 sebagian karena koneksi ke Sora.

### Core Wound

**The Split Self:** "yang memilih bertahan / dan yang seharusnya mati."

Inside The Void, Hasan sees himself divided — the version who survived and the version who should have died. This is not metaphor. The Void reflects, and what Hasan saw was two versions of himself. One persisted. One should not exist. He does not know which one he is.

Later, Zero takes him: "AIR IS FORMLESS. FORMLESS ENABLES ERROR." Hasan's last words: "Aku tidak bisa tetap berbentuk." He is taken — "ditarik keluar dari realitas" — by the Void's interface. His formlessness is both his nature and his vulnerability.

**Secondary wounds:**
- Married to Sevraya — a woman who "berfungsi, tidak hidup." The marriage is real but distant.
- Prior relationship with NiuNiu — unresolved. She sits in his lap. She has stolen the Eye of The Void from him. The dynamic is power, not romance.
- Connected to Sora — Sora is "katalis." The nature of this connection is not documented.

### Invocation History

Hasan participates in exactly ONE boot sequence phase, then is removed:

| Phase | Invocation | Role | Outcome |
|-------|-----------|------|---------|
| 1 — VOID ENTRY | Vesica Piscis (Timer 0700) | Provider + participant | The Merge formed |
| — | ABSENT Timer 1800–2500 | Not among six sigils | Not in Void Lock, Chain, Paradox |
| — | Taken by Zero (Timer 1300) | Victim | "Ditarik keluar dari realitas" |

**Pattern:** Hasan enters at Phase 1 and is removed before Phase 2. He is the only Merge participant who does not continue through the boot sequence. His structural role is: provide the apparatus, participate in the entry, form the triadic connection, then be taken — creating the vacancy Gwaneum fills.
