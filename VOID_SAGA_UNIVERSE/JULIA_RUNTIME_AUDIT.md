## STEP 3 — Runtime Compression Candidate

### JULIA RUNTIME NOTES

**Runtime Class:** Soldier → Navigator → Mother → Cosmic Function

**Primary Contradiction:**
She survived when her team didn't. The survival is the wound. Every act of tactical competence is proof she should have died too. She cannot stop being a soldier because being a soldier is the only explanation for why she lived. But the explanation is false — Void Lock shattered Heroism. She didn't survive because she was strong. She survived, and the survival itself became the prison.

**Evidence:** Timer 0100 (team wipe), Timer 0700 (Dayan as living wound), Timer 1800 ("YOU ARE NOT A HERO. YOU ARE THE PRISON."), LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md (tactical distance as primary defense)

**Fear:**
That her survival has no meaning. That the five people who died left only behavioral traces — and those traces are the only record that they existed. That she will also be reduced to a trace. That the soldier identity is the only self she has.

**Evidence:** Timer 0100 (behavioral traces), LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md (chain attacks soldier identity), Timer 0700 (Dayan as wound)

**Desire:**
To make the survival mean something. To ensure the five dead are witnessed. To stop being the Sergeant and become something else — without betraying what the Sergeant protected.

**Evidence:** Timer 0300 (15 years post-Dayan, navigator not soldier, raising Delphie), Timer 1200 (Trinitas 2: The Merge as witness structure), NODE_PROTOCOL.md (Julia feels Sevraya — cross-Merge witness)

**Blind Spot:**
She cannot perceive her own personhood outside of tactical function. The chain forced her to feel loss as a person — this was a breakthrough, not a baseline. Her default mode is: process everything as mission parameters. This makes her highly effective and emotionally illegible to herself.

**Evidence:** LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md (tactical distance, chain attacks soldier identity), SIGIL_ACTIVATION_PROTOCOL.md (combat reflex as sigil-adjacent automatic response)

**Residue Load (7 items):**
1. Behavioral traces of dead team (ghost) [E]
2. The Merge (echo → connection) [E]
3. Shattered Heroism illusion (wound) [E]
4. Wound-as-mouth (wound → organ) [E]
5. Cross-Merge sensory (echo → capability) [E]
6. Paradox vector marking (wound) [I]
7. Accumulated memory loss from sigil activation (wound) [PC]

**Protocol Participation (5 of 6 boot phases):**
1. Void Entry (Vesica Piscis) — Phase 1
2. Sigil Activation (forced — Void Lock) — Phase 2
3. Living Chain — Phase 3
4. Node II (indirect, via The Merge) — Phase 4
5. Paradox Deployment — Phase 5

**Failure State:**
Julia's failure state is the Heroism illusion before it was shattered: believing her survival was earned. Operating indefinitely as Sergeant. Never feeling the loss she carries. The chain broke this open. The failure state is relapse — returning to tactical framing as the only available self.

**Evidence:** Timer 1800 (illusion shattered), LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md (chain attacks soldier identity), Timer 2400 (paradox vector — integrated residue deployed, not regressed)

**Terminal State:**
Julia at the end of canon (Timer 2400 → Timer 2500): paradox vector. Wound-as-mouth — the stab she delivered to Sevraya became an organ of speech. The Merge connection intact. The five dead witnessed through her continued existence. She is no longer the Sergeant who survived. She is the structure that the survival produced.

**Evidence:** Timer 2400 (paradox deployment), Timer 2500 (post-canon), LIVING_CHAIN_PROTOCOL.md (wound-as-mouth residue), NiuNiu.runtime.md (protection across 25 Timers)
