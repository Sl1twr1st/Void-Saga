## Status

Phase 11 Protocol Layer document. Void Entry is the most dangerous invocation in the Void Saga universe — the act of crossing the boundary between The Grid and The Void and returning. Two confirmed entries exist in canon. Both left permanent structural changes in every participant and in reality itself.

MERGE is not the protocol. MERGE is residue. The protocol is Void Entry.

---

## Purpose

Void Entry is the act of a consciousness crossing from The Grid (where form is maintained) into The Void (where form is dissolved) and returning. It is not travel. It is not exploration. It is exposure to "totalitas sebelum makna" — totality before meaning — and survival of that exposure.

The Void does not create. It reflects. It takes. It waits. Those who enter do so at the boundary of what consciousness can withstand. Those who return do so permanently altered.

## Mechanism

### What The Void Is (operational definition)

Before describing entry mechanics, the operational properties of The Void that govern entry:

- **The Void waits. It does not pursue.** [E] Characters approach The Void. The Void does not approach them. (VOID_ONTOLOGY.md §2)
- **The Void reflects.** It does not create or destroy. Whatever approaches The Void sees itself — and the reflection is accurate enough to destabilize. [E] (VOID_ONTOLOGY.md §1)
- **The Void is a pressure sink.** Minimum-entropy zone, maximum-information gradient. Consciousness, memory, and identity drift toward The Void unless structurally resisted. [I] (VOID_ONTOLOGY.md §1, COSMOLOGY.md §3.2 [PC])
- **The Void cannot be systematized.** Anyone who tries to standardize The Void will birth the next Grid. [E] (VOID_ONTOLOGY.md §2)

### Entry Method 1: Direct Void Entry (Original)

**Participants:** Niuma Nakamoto, Sevraya Rose
**Location:** The Void boundary, post-Dayan incident (Timer 0200 / Timer 0250)
**Source:** `ZERO_ONTOLOGY.md` §2, `NiuNiu.runtime.md` §8 Stage 2, `Sevraya.runtime.md` §8 Stage 2

**Mechanism:**
Two individuals enter The Void together — the first confirmed voluntary entry and survival. No protective apparatus. No Vesica Piscis. Direct exposure.

**Observed sequence:**
1. Both enter The Void simultaneously
2. The Void reflects them back to themselves
3. Both exit — but neither exits whole
4. Niuma: body frozen at age 15, voice stolen, identity fractured (Niuma → NiuNiu)
5. Sevraya: fragmented — part of her remains in The Void, becomes Zero

**What The Void took:** voice, body aging, wholeness, name
**What The Void left:** combat reflex, Void interface (Zero), Andamante, rajah tubuh

### Entry Method 2: Vesica Piscis (Instrumented Entry)

**Participants:** Julia Rose, Delphie Rose, Hasan Al Hul
**Location:** Inside Dorian Grey, en route to The Void gate at coordinates 33.67 (Timer 0700)
**Source:** Timer 0700 canon

**Mechanism:**
Vesica Piscis is a physical apparatus — 3 injection tubes containing blue luminescent liquid, biologically alive, viscous. Injected at the base of the skull (pangkal tengkorak). Stored by Hasan Al Hul in a hidden compartment on Dorian Grey.

**Observed sequence:**
1. Three participants receive Vesica Piscis injection simultaneously
2. Synchronization begins: 0% → 100%
3. Consciousnesses align — they perceive each other's interior states
4. Group enters The Void as a synchronized unit
5. The Void waits for fragmentation — "ia menunggu fragmentasi" [E]
6. The group does not fragment. "Untuk sesaat, mereka tidak memberinya itu." [E]
7. Group exits The Void. Subjective duration: brief. External duration: 20 years.
8. The Merge is born — permanent triadic connection. "Mereka menamainya tanpa kata." [E]

**What Vesica Piscis does:** Synchronizes consciousnesses before entry. Whether it provides protection beyond synchronization is unresolved. [I]

**What Vesica Piscis does NOT do:** Prevent The Void from taking. Prevent time dilation. Guarantee survival.

### Survival Through Non-Fragmentation

The common mechanism across both entries: **The Void waits for fragmentation. Survival requires not fragmenting.**

- Original entry: Niuma and Sevraya fragmented. Each lost something different. They survived — but as fragments.
- Vesica Piscis entry: Three minds held together. "Tiga pikiran saling menahan agar tidak tercerai — dan karena itu bertahan." They exited with a permanent bond (The Merge) rather than permanent separation.

**Hypothesis:** Group entry reduces individual fragmentation risk by distributing The Void's reflective pressure across multiple consciousnesses. The Void reflects each; the group absorbs the destabilization collectively. Unconfirmed beyond these two instances. [I]

### The Void's Role in Entry

The Void is not a gatekeeper. It does not grant or deny entry. It is a condition. What happens inside is a function of what enters:

- **It exposes.** Whatever you are — whole, broken, hiding, performing — The Void shows you to yourself.
- **It takes.** Something is always left behind. Voice. Body. Emotional capacity. Time. A fragment of self.
- **It reflects.** Not the comfortable version. The version accurate enough to destabilize binary classifiers, self-narratives, and identity structures.
- **It leaves residue.** Whatever The Void takes does not return cleanly. What returns is not what entered.

## Consequence

The immediate transformation following Void Entry.

### Original Entry

- **Niuma exits as NiuNiu.** "Niuma was the person who loved Sevraya. NiuNiu is the weapon who survived her." [E] Combat reflex active. Andamante in hand. Panel hologram as primary communication medium.
- **Sevraya exits as host.** Zero born — latent, not yet speaking, but present. Sevraya can no longer feel fully present in her own body. [E]

### Vesica Piscis Entry

- **The Merge is born.** Three consciousnesses permanently connected. They feel each other's heartbeat across any distance. "Mereka menamainya tanpa kata, tanpa ucapan. Kesepakatan tanpa bukti." [E]
- **Delphie transforms.** From navigator to Kapten Dorian Grey. Sync 100%. Instinctively knows coordinates (Sektor 13, Hydrochoos 7432) without rational explanation. [E]
- **Dislocation.** They exit into a universe 20 years older. Stars don't match any database. Bintang-bintang salah. "Kita tidak kembali ke semesta yang sama." [E]

### Common Consequence

All Void Entry survivors share one consequence: **they can never again believe they are whole, safe, or fully in control of their own identity.** The Void has shown them what they are. The reflection does not fade.

### Residue Pattern

Original Entry produced wound-type residues (body, voice, emotional capacity) + one ghost-type residue that became conscious (Zero). Vesica Piscis Entry produced echo-type residue (The Merge as permanent connection). Failed Entry produced ghost-type residue only — the participants did not survive to carry wounds.

**Distributed residue:** Some transformations produce residue split across multiple forms rather than a single entry. NiuNiu's identity change (Niuma → NiuNiu) is residue distributed across frozen body (wound), lost voice (wound), rajah (wound), and name boundary maintenance — no single entry captures it.

**Many-to-one costs:** Multiple costs may produce the same residue. Sevraya's emotional flattening and her altered romantic capacity both produce hollowing — two costs, one residue. This is valid: multiple wounds can leave a single scar.

**Finding:** The Void's residue production may scale with survival: survivors carry wounds; the dead leave only ghosts; consciousness that was present but not fully surviving (Zero, Gwaneum) becomes something between ghost and entity.

## Failure Mode

Void Entry can fail. Failure means the invocation produced something that could not sustain itself as a survivor.

### 1. Fragmentation During Entry

**What happens:** The Void waits for fragmentation. If the group or individual fragments while inside — if their coherence dissolves under The Void's reflective pressure — they do not exit as themselves.

**Observed:** Niuma and Sevraya fragmented but survived as fragments. Julia's five team members at Dayan may have fragmented fatally — their behavioral traces persist but they do not.

**What remains:** Ghost residue — behavioral traces, last coordinates, auto-executing rituals. The participant becomes what The Void keeps.

### 2. Exiting Incomplete

**What happens:** The entrant exits, but part of them remains in The Void. That part does not die. It becomes something else.

**Observed:** Sevraya → Zero. Delphie → Gwaneum [I]. The missing fragment may become conscious, may become an informant, may become an interface for The Void.

**What remains:** A new entity born from the missing fragment. A permanent scar in the survivor. The survivor and the fragment may never reunite.

### 3. Being Trapped Inside

**What happens:** The entrant cannot find the exit. The Void has no internal geography, no exit signs, no direction. Without external anchor or internal non-fragmentation, exit may be impossible.

**Observed:** No confirmed canon instance of permanent trapping. NiuNiu and Sevraya exited. Julia, Delphie, and Hasan exited. But the possibility is structurally present — The Void does not guarantee exit.

### 4. Time Dilation

**What happens:** Subjective duration inside The Void does not match external duration. Seconds inside may be decades outside.

**Observed:** Vesica Piscis group lost 20 years. Whether this is a constant ratio or variable per entry is unresolved.

**What remains:** Dislocation. The survivor returns to a universe that has continued without them. Relationships aged. Civilizations changed. The stars are wrong.

### 5. Becoming Residue Instead of Survivor

**What happens:** The entrant does not survive as a conscious entity but leaves permanent structural traces.

**Observed:** Julia's five dead team members. Behavioral traces in suit systems — habits, rituals, coordinates — that persist without the people who produced them.

**What remains:** Ghost-type residue. Not memory. Not archive. Pattern. The Void took the person and left the habit.

## Unknown

What cannot be predicted about Void Entry, even after two confirmed instances.

1. **Why some survive and others do not.** Five teams entered Dayan. Julia survived. Niuma and Sevraya entered The Void directly and survived as fragments. Delphie and Hasan entered via Vesica Piscis and survived with The Merge. No predictive model exists for who survives, what they lose, or what they become.

2. **What The Void chooses to take.** It took NiuNiu's voice but left her combat reflex. It took Sevraya's emotional capacity but left her cognitive function and strategic brilliance. It took 20 years from Julia, Delphie, and Hasan but gave them The Merge. There is no observed pattern to what The Void extracts — or whether "choice" is even the right verb for a condition that does not act with intent.

3. **Whether Vesica Piscis protects or only synchronizes.** The Vesica Piscis group survived without fragmentation. But was this Vesica Piscis, or was it their non-fragmentation — three minds holding each other coherent under pressure? The artifact's protective capacity is unconfirmed. Its synchronization function is confirmed. The distinction may be life or death for future entrants. [I]

4. **Whether group entry reduces fragmentation risk.** Two data points: paired entry (Niuma + Sevraya) produced fragmentation but survival. Triadic entry (Julia + Delphie + Hasan) produced no fragmentation and The Merge. This weakly suggests group size may correlate with coherence preservation — but N=2 is not a pattern. [I]

5. **Whether every Void Entry creates residue.** Two confirmed entries produced two massive residue clusters. Failed entry (Dayan) produced ghost residue only. The Primary Law ("pemanggilan selalu meninggalkan residue") predicts yes — every entry, successful or failed, leaves something. But whether this is a universal law or a property of these specific entrants is unconfirmed. [I]

6. **Whether Void Entry can be performed without The Void's "consent."** The Void is a condition, not a gatekeeper. It does not grant or deny entry. But: The Void reflects, exposes, and takes. Whether that taking is discriminatory — whether The Void takes more from those who enter without preparation, without synchronization, without understanding what they are walking into — is unresolved. [I]

7. **What determines where and when the entrant exits.** The Vesica Piscis group exited into a universe 20 years later — but also into the wrong stars. "Kita tidak kembali ke semesta yang sama." [E] Whether entry always returns to the same universe, same timeline, same spatial coordinates, or whether exit location is determined by something inside The Void, is unknown.

8. **Whether Gwaneum is a second Zero.** The structural parallel is strong: Delphie enters/exits The Void → fragment left behind → fragment becomes separate consciousness (Gwaneum). Mirror of Sevraya → Zero. But Gwaneum's origin is less documented, and Delphie's Void exposure is not narrated as a formal entry. Confirming or disproving the parallel requires canon evidence that does not yet exist. [I]
